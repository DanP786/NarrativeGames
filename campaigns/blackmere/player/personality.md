# How the world sees Liane

## In general
A debtor's daughter, taken in settlement — a humiliation to some, a curiosity to others, a lever to a few. Nobody at Blackmere yet knows what she *is* to the master, which makes her dangerous to be unkind to and dangerous to be kind to. The household watches her the way you watch a coin someone dropped and hasn't picked up: is it yours to take, or a trap?

## At Blackmere
- The staff fear Casimir and have learned that his moods cost them. Liane is an unsolved variable in that arithmetic. Some will be coldly correct; some will probe; at least one will resent that the master is gentle with her and no one else.
- Her noble birth means the forms are (barely) observed — she is "my lady" to her face — but everyone knows the forms are paper over a debt.

## By faction
- **House Vael / the household:** undecided, watchful, some resentful.
- **The Greywater Syndicate:** she was a line in a ledger they sold. They have no further interest unless she becomes one again.
- **Her own family:** depends on her conduct. Her standing — and her sister's — rises or falls with how she carries this.
