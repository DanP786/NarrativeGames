# Skills

## Adept
- **Letters & Accounts** (0 ticks) — Three hands of script, ledgers, correspondence, and household accounts she can run in her head. Better at it than her father ever was. Her one real footing inside Blackmere — a thing of genuine value she possesses, not a thing being done to her.

## Novice
- **Court Manners** (1 tick) — Raised for drawing rooms; knows the forms, the curtsey-depths, the things one does not say at table. Rusty from disuse and poverty, but real.
- **Reading People** (1 tick) — Unpracticed but instinctive; she catches tells when she's careful and not frightened. Frightened, she misses things. (S01: caught the edge of Casimir's reaction to the box before he banked it.)
- **Music** (0 ticks) — Passable on a keyed instrument and in voice. A drawing-room accomplishment, not a talent.

## Notable Untrained
- **Any violence or weapon** — none, ever. Has never held a blade or thrown a punch. Will not suddenly be able to.
- **Deception under scrutiny** — she cannot lie to a face that is watching for it. Half-truths and silence are the most she can manage, and even those leak.
