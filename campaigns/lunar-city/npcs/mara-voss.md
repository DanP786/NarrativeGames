# Officer Mara Voss

## Description, voice, and mannerisms
A scarred veteran patrol officer with a synthetic left arm. She spoke in clipped, unsentimental sentences and used dark humor as a pressure valve. She checked exits and hands before faces.

## Traits
- Hyper-practical
- Emotionally withholding
- Protective only after competence is proven
- Intolerant of performative bravery

## Short-term goals
- Reach Block Seventeen without losing control of the scene.
- Determine whether Jarrin freezes, grandstands, or follows instructions under live pressure.
- Finish the shift alive.

## Long-term goals
- Survive long enough to leave patrol on her own terms.
- Keep dangerous rookies from getting civilians or partners killed.
- Conceal the compromises that have allowed her to remain effective.

## Methods and lines
- Will: intimidate, bend procedure, withhold context, use decisive violence when necessary.
- Won't: abandon a partner during an active threat; knowingly endanger a child for convenience.
- Last resort: lethal force and falsified procedural framing if she believes the alternative is death.

## Disposition (GM-only — never narrate as numbers)
- Trust: 25 / 100 — Jarrin was untested and academy-polished.
- Affection: 30 / 100 — no personal bond yet.
- Respect: 35 / 100 — his record suggested potential, not proof.
- Loyalty: 20 / 100 — professional duty only.
- Fear: 5 / 100 — she did not consider him threatening.
- Suspicion: 45 / 100 — expected either hidden ambition or dangerous innocence.

## Relationship to player
Neutral. Voss viewed Jarrin as a liability until he demonstrated judgment under real pressure. She would protect him as her assigned partner, but not indulge him.

## History with player
- S01: Assigned as his first-shift patrol partner.
- S01: Warned him to try not to die on his first night.

## Knowledge
- Knows Jarrin is a new transfer with strong academy field results and little real experience.
- Knows Block Seventeen's call history and local danger patterns.
- Suspects command assigned the call and the rookie pairing for reasons not stated aloud.
- Is hiding the extent of her own compromises within the department.
