# Jarrin

## Identity
Jarrin was a young, inexperienced police officer newly transferred to Lunar City. He had moved with his young family and wanted a steady income, a safer future for them, and a chance to prove that he belonged in the field.

## Background
He had performed well in most academy field exercises. Training had rewarded his physical readiness, observation, and willingness to act. It had not prepared him for a city where procedure, corruption, and survival often occupied the same uniform.

## Strengths
- Accomplished in practical field exercises.
- Keen to prove himself.
- Physically capable and observant under pressure.
- Still possessed a clear moral line.

## Flaws and gaps
- Inexperienced in live urban policing.
- Naïve about institutional corruption and criminal influence.
- Initially unwilling to follow Lunar City's unofficial rules.
- Carries the pressure of supporting a young family.

## Current state
On his first shift, riding with veteran Officer Mara Voss toward Block Seventeen after a domestic disturbance escalated into shots fired and possible gang involvement.

## Goals
- Survive each shift and return home.
- Make ends meet for his family.
- Prove himself as a police officer.
- Preserve his principles in a city designed to erode them.
