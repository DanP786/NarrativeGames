# Tone and Rules

- Genre: Cyberpunk noir thriller.
- Tone: Dark, gritty, tense, morally compromised, and fast-moving.
- Content posture: Graphic violence is permitted. No listed content is categorically off-limits.
- Agency posture: Gritty realism. Competence matters, but inexperience, bad preparation, institutional power, and lethal circumstances can produce failure.
- Death rule: Hard death enabled. Jarrin can die in genuinely lethal circumstances.
- Mind-reading rule: No telepathy, magical empathy, or direct technological access to private thoughts. NPCs react only to witnessed actions, spoken words, and plausible surface tells.
- Voice: Third person.
- Tense: Past.
- Prose style: Fast-paced tech thriller. Cinematic and action-forward, using clean modern language, tight scene movement, strong dialogue, and selective sensory detail. Investigations, pursuits, hacks, conspiracies, and violence drive the pace. The prose should not become lush, leisurely, or heavily metaphorical.
- Resolution: No dice and no exposed probabilities. Outcomes follow skill, preparation, equipment, pressure, and circumstance.
