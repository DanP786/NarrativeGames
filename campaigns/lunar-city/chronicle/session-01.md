# Session 01 — The Night Transfer

Rain hammered the maglev as Jarrin arrived in Lunar City for his first police shift. The city appeared as a forest of steel, neon, advertisements, and permanent darkness. He carried one promise from his wife into the precinct: "Just come home."

Lunar Central Precinct was overloaded and cynical. Before Jarrin could settle in, command paired him with veteran Officer Mara Voss for a domestic disturbance in Block Seventeen. Voss, scarred and equipped with a synthetic left arm, handed him the patrol cruiser access and warned him, "Try not to die on your first night."

While they were en route, dispatch upgraded the call: shots fired, civilians fleeing, possible gang involvement. The patrol cruiser accelerated toward Block Seventeen as Voss observed that the routine call had changed.
