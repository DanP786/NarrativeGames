# The Rosier Vault, Gringotts

## Description
A high-security vault, deep beneath Diagon Alley, older than the bank's modern numbering. Carved out before the goblins kept ledgers in writing.

Two chambers. The front holds the gold heaps, plate, family papers, and a row of cabinets along the side wall (jewellery, genealogy, miscellany). The back chamber is older — a low stone bench with the family crest cut into its face, three iron sconces, a tall narrow case of black-spined unmarked books, and a glass dome on a plinth (currently empty; clean dust-ring).

## Access
- Combined goblin-and-Rosier-blood opening. Family members of age can enter solo with the key and a goblin attendant.
- One blood-sealed drawer in the genealogy cabinet base — Rosier blood only. Wax seal re-forms when shut, with a faint hairline if examined closely.

## Current state (as of 1 Sep 1998 morning)
- Cabinets are open. Items missing per Brookhouse's last-Yule inventory: the Crisanthemum Set (jewellery), three genealogy volumes, the reliquary that sat on the back plinth.
- Footprints in dust at the back: a woman's, light tread. Walks in to the plinth; does not return.
- Cass has signed a Ministry petition; an inventory is due within a fortnight, with Brookhouse attending.

## Sealed drawer contents (left in place by Cass)
- Plain brass key (unknown lock, no enchantment Cass could feel)
