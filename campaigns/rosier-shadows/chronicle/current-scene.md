# Current scene — Platform 9¾ / Hogwarts Express (1 Sep 1998, ~10:50–14:00)

## Beats
- Cass arrives via Floo at King's Cross, 10:32. Walks through the barrier alone.
- Platform crush: Gryffindor seventh-years near the front (Ginny Weasley with her mother), a tight cluster of parentless Slytherin first-years near the rear, Daphne and Astoria Greengrass in the middle.
- Daphne notices Cass — small chin-tilt, neither greeting nor cut. Cass approaches to three feet; does not speak first.
- Daphne wins the silence game; Cass does not break first either. Daphne uses "Cassie" — first time anyone outside Brookhouse has, since the funeral.
- Daphne offers a seat in the carriage: "Last carriage but two, the side facing forward. Pansy and Millicent will be along; Tracey is travelling with the Ravenclaws this year, apparently."
- Daphne sends Astoria for pumpkin pasties — small kindness disguised as an errand, freeing the conversation of younger ears.
- Daphne, alone with Cass: "My mother sends her sympathies. She told me to tell you we did not attend the funeral because we were not invited. She wanted you to know that. Was that on purpose. Or did Brookhouse forget."
- Cass: "I couldn't say, my parents left instructions in their will. You didn't miss much, the Ministry being there made it even more uncomfortable." (Half-truth — she does not know if the will included such instructions; she is covering.)
- Daphne hears the seam, accepts the cover anyway. Soft beat: "I am sorry about your mother. She was kind to me once, when I was twelve."
- Daphne arranges the carriage seating: Cass at window, Astoria beside her, Daphne beside Astoria, Pansy and Millicent across. Notes that Pansy has been told to "manage herself this term."
- Astoria returns with pumpkin pasties; offers one to Cass before her sister — small, deliberate breach of protocol.
- Second whistle. They turn toward the train.
- Corridor: third-year mixed-house compartment falls silent (the afraid). Gryffindor 5th-year boy with sandy hair and a scar across the nose, foot in door: *"Walk faster, Rosier. We've got OWLs on this train."* Girl beside him: "Leave it. She's not worth the breath."
- Slytherin 1st-year compartment: 8-9 packed in, parentless. One small one with too-long hair and pale eyes gives Cass a tiny nod. Cass does not return it.
- Carriage: Pansy and Millicent already seated. Pansy's hostile look is bitten back — she has clearly been told to behave. Closes her face, turns to window. Daphne, calm: "Sit." Cass takes the window seat. Astoria beside her. Millicent silently shares sandwich-paper across the gap.
- Cass walks alongside Astoria toward the carriage. Asks low: "How was your summer?" Astoria, after a beat: family kept gates closed all summer, kept past the orchard wall. Father took Daphne to two trials "to be seen sitting"; refused to take Astoria. Astoria wrote Cass a letter in July, mother said it was "premature," she did not send it. "I don't think it was premature. I'm sorry your summer was —" Cass: "Thanks." Walks down the train.
- Tracey Davis joins the carriage after the trolley's third pass. Loud, half-blood, sharp-tongued. Came back for year-mates rather than NEWTs.
- Daphne and Tracey carry the carriage conversation. Astoria sleeps against the glass.
- After ~1 hour Cass excuses herself to the toilet, locks the cubicle, opens the letter.
- **Letter contents (canon — letter destroyed but Cass knows):**
  - "I have not come back" — written in expectation of dying. Mother does not apologise for being a Death Eater; tells Cass to reach her own conclusions honestly.
  - Points Cass to the blood-sealed drawer in the vault back-cabinet (which Cass has already opened).
  - Brass key: take it.
  - Cipher note: written in the family substitution. Mother says **Brookhouse holds the third cipher key**; he has had cause to use it "once before." He will give it to Cass when he sees she has already opened the drawer.
  - The location named in the cipher (third word — currently unreadable to Cass without Brookhouse's key): "the place." A custodian is there.
  - **The custodian:** "the one who keeps it has known you since you were a child, though you will not remember her." Cass cannot place the woman.
  - **Recognition protocol:** Show the rose-and-thorn. Speak the parents' names (Argus and Selene). The custodian will give Cass "what I cannot."
  - Closing: "I have loved you with a pride that frightened me sometimes." Burn instruction.
- Cass burns the letter (Incendio, controlled), washes the ash with Aguamenti. Returns to carriage.
- Cass settles back at the window. Three and a half hours to Hogsmeade.
