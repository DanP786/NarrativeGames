# Session 01 — 9 May 2026 (real-world)

## Scene 1 — The Rosier Vault, Gringotts (1 Sep 1998, morning)
Cass meets Brookhouse, the family solicitor, in the deep Rosier vault on the morning of her return to Hogwarts. Three documents on the table: routine trust paperwork, a Ministry petition requesting consent to inventory the vault for "items of dark provenance," and a sealed letter from her mother bearing the rose-and-thorn seal, held by Brookhouse for four months. Cass signs the trust paperwork without comment and asks Brookhouse to attend the inventory; he agrees, having done two such inventories already this summer.

Walking the vault, she finds cabinets open and items missing since Brookhouse's last attended visit at Yule — the Crisanthemum Set, three genealogies, and the reliquary that sat on the back plinth. Footprints in the dust at the back: a woman's tread, light, walking in to the empty plinth and not returning. With the bone-handled family dagger she opens a blood-sealed drawer in the genealogy cabinet base. Inside a black lacquered box keyed to her blood: a plain brass key (unknown lock, no felt enchantment), a parchment in the Rosier cipher reading **Hollowfield / Rookery / [unreadable third word]**, and an enamel rose set into the lid dated **28 April 1998** — four days before her mother's death. She closes the drawer without taking anything, signs the petition with Brookhouse's added stipulation, and pockets her mother's letter unopened. Brookhouse, parting: *"I am at the firm any night you need me."*
