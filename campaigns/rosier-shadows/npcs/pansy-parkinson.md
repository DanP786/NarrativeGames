# Pansy Parkinson

## Status
Alive. 18. Eighth-year Slytherin — returnee for the disrupted Carrow year. Two years above Cass.

## Description
Sharp, beautiful, a face built for cruelty when it wants to be one. Very dark hair worn deliberately. Always sees herself in glass first, the room second.

## Voice & mannerisms
Cutting in private. In public this term — measured, on a tight rein. The rein is visible if you know to look.

## Relationship to Cass
**Wary** (provisional, leaning hostile). Pansy and Cass have no direct prior history beyond shared House. Pansy's first instinct on the train is open hostility; she is suppressing it on someone's instructions. She has been "told the family expects her to manage herself this term" (per Daphne).

## Goals & pressures
- Get out of Hogwarts with NEWTs and her family standing intact.
- Almost certainly under some form of behavioural mandate from the Parkinson family — she famously suggested handing Harry over to Voldemort during the Battle of Hogwarts, which is now public knowledge and a stain.
- Whether the leash is solely family, or also Ministry-imposed, is unclear.

## History with Cass
- Shared House. No close prior connection.
- 1 Sep 1998 (carriage): hostile look bitten back. No greeting offered.

## Knowledge
- Whatever a senior Slytherin knows about a junior Slytherin's family.
- Does not (presumably) know the contents of the vault, the cipher note, the letter.
