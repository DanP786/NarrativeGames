# Astoria Greengrass

## Status
Alive. 15. Fifth-year Slytherin — Cass's year and dormitory.

## Description
Newly grown out of childhood, hair longer this year than last, still trying on the version of herself her older sister wears so easily. Quicker to smile than Daphne. Less practised.

## Voice & mannerisms
Speaks readily, then catches herself when Daphne is in earshot. Watches Cass with the open curiosity of someone who has not yet decided to be guarded.

## Relationship to Cass
**Friendly.** Probable warmest connection in the dormitory. Astoria's small nod on the platform was the first warm thing anyone said to Cass, in any form, since she walked through the barrier. She also offered Cass a pasty before her sister — a deliberate, small breach of protocol that Daphne almost certainly saw.

## Goals & pressures
- Get on with her year. O.W.L.s loom.
- Be the version of a Greengrass her sister and mother want her to be, while privately wanting something less calculated.

## History with Cass
- Dorm-mates fourth year (1997-98). Did not interact much; Cass kept to herself the second half of the year, and Astoria was a year-mate she'd had no particular reason to seek out.
- 1 Sep 1998 (platform): tried to greet Cass but was checked by Daphne's hand on her wrist. Sent for sandwiches as a pretext.
- 1 Sep 1998 (corridor, train): confided to Cass that her family was kept shut behind the orchard wall all summer; that her father took Daphne to two trials "to be seen sitting"; that she wrote Cass a letter in July and her mother told her not to send it; that she did not think it was premature; that she was sorry for Cass's summer.

## Knowledge
- Knows what every Slytherin knows about Cass: name, parents, the dungeons-during-the-battle story, the silence that followed.
- Does not know about the vault, the letter, or anything Daphne is privately discussing with Cass.
