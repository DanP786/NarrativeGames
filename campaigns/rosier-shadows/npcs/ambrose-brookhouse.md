# Ambrose Brookhouse, Esq.

## Status
Alive. Senior partner at Brookhouse, Wattle & Pyne — old Diagon Alley legal firm, family solicitors to several pure-blood houses including the Rosiers.

## Description
Sixties. Neat grey beard, the slight stoop of a man who has read his way through forty years. Robes a touch too fine for working hours — a habit, not vanity. Quiet voice. Reads a room before he speaks in it.

## Voice & mannerisms
Old-fashioned formality with clients in public. Drops to "Cassie" only when alone with her, and only when something matters. Pauses before delivering bad news. Folds documents with both hands.

## Relationship to Cass
**Friendly.** Has known her since she was a child. Served her grandfather. Genuinely cares about her — possibly the only adult left who knew her parents and still calls her by her childhood name. Will not lie to her. Will not always tell her everything, but will signal when he is withholding.

## Goals & pressures
- Honour his obligations to the Rosier estate while keeping the firm clear of legal trouble in the post-war Ministry climate.
- Has done two estate inventories already this summer for other Death Eater-adjacent families. Knows what the Ministry takes and how. Tired of it.
- Some private guilt about the Rosiers — what he did or did not see across forty years of service.

## History with Cass
- Forty years as Rosier solicitor; present at her grandfather's death.
- Last attended the vault with Argus Rosier "last Yule" (Dec 1997).
- Was the one who handled the funeral arrangements after the Battle of Hogwarts. Held her mother's sealed letter for four months until Cass came of age to receive it.
- 1 Sep 1998: vault meeting at Gringotts. Walked her through Ministry petition; agreed to attend the inventory. Did not press her on what she found in the back of the vault.

## Knowledge
- Knows the working inventory of the Rosier vault as of last Yule.
- Does NOT have access to the goblin entry logs (post-war, no court orders being honoured).
- Did not previously know about the blood-sealed drawer in the back. Has now seen Cass open it. Did not look at the contents. Will not ask about them unless she raises it.
- Has noticed the open cabinets and missing items himself; was already troubled when Cass arrived.
- **Holds the third Rosier cipher key** (per Selene Rosier's letter). Has had cause to use it once before. Per the letter's instruction, will give Cass the third reading "when he sees you have already opened the drawer." Cass has now opened it; she has not yet contacted him about this.
