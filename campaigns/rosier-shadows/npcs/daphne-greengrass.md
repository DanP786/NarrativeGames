# Daphne Greengrass

## Status
Alive. 18. Eighth-year Slytherin — voluntarily returned for the disrupted Carrow year, sitting NEWTs this June. Two years above Cass.

## Description
Beautifully turned out, always. Pale, fair-haired, composed. Reads a room with the same precision Cass is learning. The kind of face you do not realise is a face until she chooses to make an expression with it.

## Voice & mannerisms
Dry, measured, slightly older than her years. Uses first names sparingly and deliberately — when she does, it means something. Long pauses she does not feel obliged to fill.

## Relationship to Cass
**Friendly** (provisional). The Greengrass family is positioning itself as old-blood-but-not-Death-Eater — neutrals who kept their gates closed during the war. Daphne's mother sent sympathies; Daphne is the messenger. Whether this becomes warmth or transactional depends on what Cass does with it.

## Goals & pressures
- Pass NEWTs cleanly and exit Hogwarts with the Greengrass name unstained.
- Quietly maintain the family's pre-war network of old-blood relationships, including the Rosiers.
- Look after Astoria, who is a year-mate of Cass's and (privately) the family's true heir.

## History with Cass
- Schoolmate two years ahead. They knew each other in passing through Slytherin and through their mothers' social circle.
- Greengrasses were not invited to the Rosier funeral; the family noted this.
- 1 Sep 1998 (platform): waited Cass out in silence, used "Cassie", offered carriage seat, asked the funeral question pointedly. Heard the seam in Cass's "will instructions" answer; let it hold. Soft beat: "I am sorry about your mother. She was kind to me once, when I was twelve."

## Knowledge
- Knows the broad shape of Argus and Selene Rosier's deaths and Death Eater ties.
- Selene Rosier was kind to her at twelve — there is some prior connection between the families' women.
- Does not know about the vault's missing items, the cipher note, or the letter.
