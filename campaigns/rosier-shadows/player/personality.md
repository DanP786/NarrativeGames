# How the world sees Cass

## Reputation in general
The Rosier name is dirt. It is not screamed at her in corridors — that would be rude, and the wizarding world is still mostly polite — but it is heard. Conversations stop when she enters a room she did not announce herself in. Strangers look at her twice. Some looks are pity. Most are not.

## By House
- **Slytherin:** Mixed. Some respect her name; some resent that she did not fight; a few of the younger ones look at her like she might know what to do.
- **Gryffindor:** Hostile to wary. Many lost family to people her parents called friends.
- **Ravenclaw:** Watchful. They want to know what she is before they decide.
- **Hufflepuff:** Pity, mostly. A few open hands. She has not yet taken any of them.

## Dormitory (5th-year Slytherin girls)
- Astoria Greengrass is a year-mate and shared the dorm last year. Quiet acquaintance, no warmth or coldness yet — opportunity in either direction.
- Other 5th-year girls TBD as they appear.

## By faction
- **The Ministry:** A name on a list. Not a suspect; not yet trusted.
- **Old-blood families:** She is the last Rosier. They are watching to see what she does with that.
- **The teachers:** Slughorn is gentle with her. McGonagall is fair. Others are still making up their minds.
