# Cassiopeia "Cass" Rosier

## Vitals
- **Age:** 15
- **Year:** Fifth-year Slytherin
- **Blood status:** Pure-blood
- **Wand:** Walnut, dragon heartstring core, 10¾ inches, slightly springy. Bought from Ollivander, summer 1994 — third wand on the counter; Ollivander remarked that walnut sometimes does that with a clever child, and that she should be careful what she asked it to do.
- **Family:** Last of the Rosier line. Parents Argus and Selene Rosier, both Death Eaters, both died at the Battle of Hogwarts. Extended family scattered or dead in earlier purges.
- **Estate:** Rosier Hall (location TBD), held in trust until her majority. Vault at Gringotts.

## Backstory
Born to old blood and raised on the certainty of it. Argus and Selene were not the loudest of Voldemort's followers, but they were faithful. Cass was at Hogwarts the night her parents died — fifteen, in the dungeons with the rest of fifth-year Slytherin, told to stay put while the castle shook. She did not fight. She did not join. She did not pick the school's side either.

The morning after the battle, Slughorn came down to the dungeons and told her, gently, that her father and mother were dead. She did not cry in front of him. The summer that followed was spent at the Hall, alone with house-elves and lawyers and a silence that did not lift.

## Current state
Quiet. Watchful. She has learned in the last four months to read a room before she enters it, and to keep her face still. She is still proud of her name, but proud now in the way of people whose pride has been tested. She is open to ideas she would have dismissed a year ago.

## Goals
- Get through fifth year and her O.W.L.s.
- Understand what her parents actually were, and what that makes her.
- Survive a school that has not decided yet whether she is a victim or a villain.
- Pursue her mother's bequest: the brass key, the cipher note, the place named **Hollowfield / Rookery / ?**, and the woman who keeps it.
