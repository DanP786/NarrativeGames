# Skills

## Adept
- **Charms** (10 ticks) — Her defining competence and her joy. Collects them like other girls collect rumours. Wandwork is precise; incantations clean. Will pick up a new charm with one good demonstration.

## Novice
- **Subterfuge** (3 ticks) — Reading a room, half-truths, knowing when to vanish. Dorm-survival in a year when half the school hates her House.
- **Dark Arts** (0 ticks) — Theoretical fluency from a pure-blood upbringing. Knows what curses do, what they cost, what they leave behind. Has used none of the worst yet. Has not ruled them out.
- **Pure-blood Lore & Etiquette** (2 ticks) — Names, sigils, the unspoken rules of old-blood gatherings. Knows whose family killed whose, three generations back.

## Notable Untrained
- **Potions** — Genuinely, embarrassingly bad. Snape's last memory of her is a sneer. New professor this year; opportunity or fresh humiliation.
- **Flying** — Dreads the Quidditch pitch. Has never flown well. Brooms do not love her.
