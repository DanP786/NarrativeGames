# Campaign setup

Created: 9 May 2026 (real-world date)
Slug: rosier-shadows

## Onboarding answers

**Genre / references:** Harry Potter, post-Battle of Hogwarts. School has reopened.

**Tone:** Shadows-of-the-war. Death Eaters at large, Ministry purges, families on the wrong side of the line. Hogwarts is rebuilt but the corridors remember.

**Content posture:** Nothing off the table.

**Stakes / scope:** Personal. Cass's fifth year, the people in her House, a story that touches Hogwarts.

**Agency posture:** Gritty realism. Failure is frequent. The world does not bend for her.

**Death rule:** Hard death enabled. The PC can die.

**Character seed:**
- Cassiopeia "Cass" Rosier, fifth-year Slytherin, pure-blood.
- Parents were Death Eaters; both died at the Battle of Hogwarts.
- She did not fight. Did not join Voldemort. Did not raise her wand for the school either. Stayed in the Slytherin dungeons with the rest of fifth-year.
- Proud of her blood, but exposure to other students has loosened her old certainties.
- Cunning. No magic off the table — good or evil — if it serves her ends.
- Was easygoing and confident before the battle; now cautious, watchful, quiet about how others receive her.
- Brilliant at Charms — collects them. Hopeless at Potions; Snape's last memory of her is exasperation.
- Awful at flying.

**Opening hook:** Rosier family vault at Gringotts, morning of 1 September 1998. Solicitor wants her to sign something. Sealed letter from her mother, unopened for four months.
