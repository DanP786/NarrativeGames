# Calendar

- **Date:** The 14th of First-Frost, in the 31st year of King Aelric Caldwyn's reign. *Night.*
- **Season:** Late autumn. The first hard frosts have come; the great lake at Caldemere is rimmed in ice but not yet sealed over. Snow is two or three weeks out.
- **Time of day:** Half past the ninth bell. Late. The Spire is quiet.
- **Sera's age:** Twenty. Her twenty-first name-day falls in late spring — roughly seven months from now. That is the Queen's deadline.
- **Notable upcoming:**
  - Tomorrow: breakfast with the Queen at the seventh bell in her small parlour.
  - Tomorrow morning: the Aerinhal mercer will be approached for a cloak in the colours Sera specified.
  - Tomorrow: the Queen will write to Lord Aelthyn at the Frostmarch about an Aelthyn daughter coming north for Eira.
  - Tomorrow third hour: Cassian Verrent paired against Marrick Ardenmark in the lists.
  - Hoarfrost Tourney runs eight more days. Closing feast in the Glasshalls on the ninth night.
  - Before the tourney closes: the Queen will ask Sera "whether you think he can stop. Your brother."
