# Campaign setup

Created: 16 May 2026 (real-world date)
Slug: the-thaw

## Onboarding answers

**Genre / references:** Medieval fantasy. Humans only. Magic is rare but more common in noble bloodlines that have married power-matches for generations. Multiple kingdoms. The reigning monarch of each kingdom is the most powerful magical figure within it — bound to the land by coronation rite.

**Voice:** 3rd person, present tense. *"Seraphina crosses the field, hand on the rail."* Held consistent regardless of how the player phrases prompts.

**Length:** Open-ended. No act tracker. The establishing arc (§16.1) still applies for the first scene-and-a-half.

**Tone:** Grounded ASOIAF-register. Moral grey is the default. Politics matter. Heroes lose. Cold but not unrelentingly cruel.

**Content posture:** Nothing off the table. Graphic violence, sexuality, torture, moral despair — on the page when the scene calls for it, not reached for.

**Stakes / scope:** Personal arc, epic stakes. The PC's choices ripple outward to dynasty- and map-level. Camera stays close.

**Geography:** One capital as anchor. Travel only if the story requires it. Wider map sketched at the level of detail a royal would actually carry.

**Agency posture:** Gritty realism. The world does not bend to keep her safe.

**Death rule:** Hard death enabled.

**Mind-reading rule:** Rare gift. One or two practitioners alive in the known world, possibly mythological. Divination, prophecy, and dream-walking sit in the same register — rare, suspect, maybe-real-maybe-charlatan.

**Main thread (sketch):** A twenty-year-old princess, third in line, has been refusing suitors for four years. Her mother has set a deadline: choose by 21 or be chosen for. Her eldest brother loves the wrong woman and is married to a kingdom. Her middle brother is a weapon she does not own. The court reads her as cold; some of that is the family magic, some is what she has frozen in herself. *What does it cost to stay cold? What does it cost to thaw?*

**Character seed:**
- Princess Seraphina ("Sera") Caldwyn of Caldros, twenty, third in line for the throne.
- Lake-white skin, long raven hair, silver-steel eyes. Slim, petite. Easy to underestimate at a distance and impossible to underestimate up close.
- Her battlefield is the court. Master of repartee, languages, etiquette, the politics of a room. Has been trained to be a queen consort and has carried the training for four years without spending it.
- Dances because she must. Sings because she wants to — privately.
- Cannot fight. Has the family ice magic but only at party-trick fluency.
- Has never kissed a man, despite four years of suitors.
- Soft places: her middle brother Daron (the Spare), and Caelum Vesper (her best friend, also her eldest brother's lover).

**Opening situation:** Tournament opening ceremony at the capital, Caldemere. The Hoarfrost Tourney has been staged this year as a marriage market. Suitors from all four kingdoms in attendance. The Queen visible. Both brothers in their respective shapes of trouble. Caelum present and legitimate. Sera one year from her mother's deadline.
