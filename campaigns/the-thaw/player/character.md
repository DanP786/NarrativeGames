# Princess Seraphina "Sera" Caldwyn

## Vitals
- **Age:** 20
- **Title:** Princess of Caldros, third in line to the Rime Throne
- **House:** Caldwyn, the cold-blooded line of the lake
- **Bloodline magic:** Ice. Has the gift in her blood; weak in expression (party-trick fluency only — see `skills.md`).
- **Family:**
  - King Aelric Caldwyn — father, holder of the Rime Throne
  - Queen Lysandra Caldwyn née Verrent — mother, originally of Aerinhal (married in as a peace bride twenty-eight years ago)
  - Crown Prince Theodric Caldwyn — eldest brother, 25, married three years
  - Princess Eira Caldwyn née Aelthyn — Theodric's wife, of the great northern fortress-house Aelthyn; has full ice magic in her own right
  - Prince Daron Caldwyn — middle brother, 22, the Spare

## Appearance
Lake-white skin. Long raven hair, usually plaited at the temples and falling loose to the small of her back. Eyes the colour of polished steel — pale grey, slightly silver in good light. Slim, petite, deceptively still. The kind of face that does not announce itself across a hall and stops a man cold once he stands close enough to look at her properly.

She dresses well, in the cold-house style: deep blues, silvers, slate-greys, the occasional white that no one else at court can wear without looking sallow. Long sleeves to the wrist year round. Two pieces of jewellery: a small steel pendant at her throat (a Caldwyn heirloom, plain) and her grandmother's silver ring on the third finger of her right hand.

## Backstory
Born twenty winters ago to the third generation of the current dynasty's hold. The marriage that produced her was itself the peace that ended the last Caldros-Aerinhal war: her mother is a Verrent princess, sent north at nineteen to seal the treaty, and the Queen has spent twenty-eight years quietly making herself indispensable in a court that initially treated her as a foreign deposit. Sera grew up watching this — the work her mother did, the price she paid, the position she ultimately won — and has drawn her own conclusions.

She was an easy child until she was about eleven. Bright, talkative, fast at her lessons, doted on by her father and brothers. Something happened around eleven or twelve — the family does not discuss it; the household staff who remember are tactful — and the easy child quieted. By thirteen she had the watchfulness she carries now. By fourteen she was already correcting tutors. By fifteen the nickname *Ice Princess* had begun to attach to her in court, though it took a year to harden into the thing it is now.

At sixteen the King and Queen offered her the unusual courtesy of choosing her own husband within reason, on the condition that the choice be made before her twenty-first name-day. Sera said yes to the courtesy and has been spending it without spending it ever since. The Queen's patience is famous but it is finite.

She has never kissed a man. Four years of suitors and not one within ten feet of her mouth. She has not explained this to anyone and no one — not her brothers, not her best friend — has asked her to.

## Current state
Twenty years old. Seven months from the deadline. Watchful, composed, very good at the work of being a princess and very tired of doing it under the eyes of men who want to bid on her. In the last six months something in her has begun to harden in a different direction — not colder, exactly, but more *decided*. She does not yet know what she has decided.

## Goals
- Get through this tourney without choosing.
- More than that: get to her name-day without being chosen *for*.
- Most than that, though she would not say it aloud: figure out what she actually wants, before someone else hands it to her in a shape she cannot refuse.
