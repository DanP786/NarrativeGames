# Skills

## Adept
- **Courtly Persuasion & Repartee** (13 ticks) — Her defining competence and the place she lives. Languages (fluent: the Caldros tongue, Aerinic, and Therondian; conversational: Ostvarn and three minor dialects), etiquette across all four crowns, table arrangements, precedence, the unspoken rules of a chamber. Reads a room in a glance. Cuts without raising her voice. Can deploy silence as a weapon. Years of refusing suitors have honed this from training into instinct.
  - *Session 1 ticks: ribbon to Cassian (pavilion, charged); the level-something through the procession (routine but sustained); the request for audience to the Queen in her own register (charged); the corridor with Cassian (climactic, twice — the half-stair offer and the goodnight).*

## Novice
- **Reading People** (4 ticks) — Knows when she's being lied to, mostly. Picks up tells. Not infallible — strong personalities can roll over her read, and the family she grew up with she is *worse* at reading than strangers, because love distorts the signal.
  - *Session 1 ticks: read her mother's chair-arrangement at the pavilion (charged); read Eira's working practice in long-knife from a single sentence about Otrim (charged).*
- **Singing** (2 ticks) — Trained, skilled, private. She has performed publicly only at the formal occasions she could not refuse and has been careful to be merely good on those occasions, not memorable. What she actually sounds like, three people in the world have heard. A public performance at full voice would be a different magnitude of risk.
- **Ice Magic — Party Tricks** (0 ticks) — Has the family gift. Can frost a glass, dim a candle by chilling the wick, leave a thin rime on the surface she touches. That is the ceiling and no amount of will has yet moved it. Why this is, no one in the family or the priesthood of the lake has yet worked out. Sera has stopped trying to push it; the trying has begun to feel like its own humiliation.

## Notable Untrained
- **Combat of any kind.** Sword, knife, bow, unarmed. A blade in her hand is a prop. She has not been taught and her body has not taught itself. The household guard would not let her near anything sharp in a real fight.
- **Battlefield ice magic.** What her brothers do — what the lowest scullion in the family's bloodline could do at her age — is closed to her. Whether this is a permanent ceiling, a frozen door, or something else is, for now, open.
- **Horsemanship beyond formal display.** She can ride in a procession. She would not survive a hard ride across country. The court does not know this because it has never come up.
