# Inventory

## Worn (formal court dress for the opening ceremony)
- Gown of dark slate-blue silk over white, long sleeves, narrow waist, the Caldwyn frost-pattern embroidered in silver thread at the hem and cuffs
- Silver-grey fur half-cloak, fastened at the shoulder with a Caldwyn rose-and-rime brooch
- Soft white leather boots, knee-high under the gown
- Plaited hair pinned with three small silver combs

## Worn (always)
- Small steel pendant at her throat on a fine chain — Caldwyn heirloom, plain, given to her by her grandmother when Sera was eight
- Grandmother's silver ring on the third finger of her right hand — single sapphire, scratched on the inside band where the engraving has worn down

## Carried
- A pair of fine kidskin gloves, tucked into her belt (court convention; she removes them indoors)
- A small embroidered purse with a few silver coins, a folded handkerchief, and a stem of dried rosemary (a habit; she does not say why)

## In her chambers at the Spire
- Wardrobe of court dress, riding habits, mourning weeds, three plain dresses for days she wishes to disappear in
- Her singing-master's lute (used in private; she also sings unaccompanied)
- A small case of books in three languages — most often re-read: a volume of Aerinic poetry, a Therondian history of the Hollow Sea wars, a treatise on hawking she does not actually care about but enjoys
- Her writing-desk: paper, ink, sealing wax, the Caldwyn personal seal she is permitted to use on letters that do not bind the crown
- A locked box at the bottom of her wardrobe (contents private; even her ladies do not know it is there)
