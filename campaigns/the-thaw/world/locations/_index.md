# Locations

*One line per location. Loaded into context every turn.*

- **Caldemere** — The capital of Caldros, built on the eastern shore of the great inland Lake Caldemere. Three districts: the Spire (the royal seat, on the headland), the Glasshalls (state-rooms and the cathedral, terraced down toward the water), and the Lower City (trade quarter, port, the markets that supply the court). See `caldemere.md` when needed.
- **The Tilting Field** — The tournament grounds on the level meadow north of the Spire, on a low rise above the lake. Stands for several thousand spectators. The royal pavilion sits at the east end facing the field. See `tilting-field.md`.
