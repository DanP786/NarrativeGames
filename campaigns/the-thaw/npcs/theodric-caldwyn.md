# Crown Prince Theodric Caldwyn

## Status
Alive. 25. Crown Prince of Caldros, heir to the Rime Throne. Married three years to Princess Eira née Aelthyn. No children yet. **Something between him and Eira shifted on the night of 14th First-Frost.** By the closing days of the Hoarfrost Tourney they were visibly sitting closer than they have in three years.

## Description
The textbook Caldwyn heir. Tall like his father, broader through the shoulders, the same long face but warmer in expression — Theodric has his mother's mouth. Black hair cut to the jaw. Silver-steel eyes. Carries himself with the easy confidence of a man who has been told, since he could walk, that the realm was already his.

A genuinely good knight, a genuinely good rider, a competent battlefield commander. Has the family ice magic at proper royal strength — not the land-binding (that comes only at coronation), but the working magic of a powerful Caldwyn lord. Can frost a yard of ground in winter; can drop the temperature in a chamber by ten degrees if he is angry; cracked a doorframe at fifteen, casually, by leaning on it during an argument with his father.

**He has a specific tilting strike — the under-the-shield, the angled lance up at the last twenty yards finding the breastplate at the hip rather than on the shield centre.** Theodric has used it in three previous tournaments and once in earnest. Cassian Verrent unhorsed Marrick Ardenmark with the strike at the third hour of the 15th of First-Frost, having read it from Theodric's published tilting commentaries. Theodric saw the strike. He did not speak through that bout. He spoke to Cassian about it in the open on the field the next morning.

## Voice & mannerisms
Easy, warm, slightly self-deprecating in private; formal in public, where he has been trained for thirty years. Laughs readily and well. Listens more than he speaks. The court generally loves him. The court generally trusts him.

The cost of being him shows in small places. He drinks more than he used to. He sleeps badly. The household has noticed the lights in his study at the second watch. *(Across the closing days of the tourney the lights have been on less. Something has eased.)*

## Goals & pressures
- Be the heir his father needs him to be. (He is. Effortlessly. This is the part of his life that is not a problem.)
- Be the husband his wife deserves. (Has been failing; **is, since the night of the 14th, trying differently.**)
- Be the man Caelum loves. (Still is. The thing has not been resolved.)
- Sire an heir. The pressure on this is mounting and is the conversation he most does not want to have.

## Relationship to Sera
**Loyal.** Older-brother fond. She is one of the two people in his life with whom he does not have to pretend. She figured out about him and Caelum before either of them did. He has never quite known how to thank her for holding the secret without ever asking for the favor that holding it normally implies.

**On the family parlour breakfast of 16th First-Frost:**
- Theodric, into his cup, on the cloak: *"Holsbrand cuts in his own house. He does not cut in twenty hours for anyone. Cassian Verrent paid in something that was not money."*
- Theodric, finally to Sera, not angrily: *"He rode well, sister. He aimed under the shield. That is one of mine."*
- *"Daron tells me he has read me twice. I would have liked to have been told that by a man before I was told it by his second pass at a strike."*
- *"I will speak to him today. Properly. In the open. — I should have done it last night at the wine."*

He spoke to Cassian later that morning on the field with Eira at his side. What was said is private. What the court read: the Crown Prince clapped the Verrent prince's shoulder publicly and kept his hand there for a count of two. The Aerinhal contingent watched and read it correctly.

**At the closing feast** he stood and lifted his cup to Sera at the dais without speaking. He did not speak through the announcement and did not need to.

## Relationship to Eira
**Mending.** **On the night of 14th First-Frost** Theodric retired to his own apartments with Eira at the ninth bell — not his pattern. What happened inside those apartments has not been told to anyone (least of all Sera). Whatever happened, by the morning of the 16th, Eira asked to come with him when he spoke to Cassian, and Theodric said *yes,* and by the closing days they were sitting close. **Eira's eyelids on the morning of the 16th were faintly raw**, but the closeness was real.

The household has noticed.

## Relationship with Caelum
Held over. By silent agreement between Lysandra, Sera, and Caelum (the three people who know), the question of *what to do about Theodric and Caelum* has been set aside through the Hoarfrost Tourney. Caelum has held the public face all nine days. Theodric and Caelum have not been seen alone together since the 14th — whether by Caelum's discipline, Theodric's, or both, is open.

The affair is not resolved. It has only been suspended.

## History with Sera
- Older brother since her birth. Doted on her as a small child.
- Was at home when the thing happened that Sera was eleven or twelve for; he was sixteen, present, did something or did not. The two of them have never spoken about it directly.
- Welcomed Caelum into his household as a sworn man-at-arms a year ago, when Caelum was knighted at twenty — at which point Sera, then nineteen, already knew Caelum better than Theodric did.
- The affair began three years ago, around the time of Theodric's marriage. He was twenty-two, Caelum was eighteen. Sera was seventeen. She knew within a season.
- **14th First-Frost** (S01): Public face through the day. Retired to his own apartments with Eira at the ninth bell.
- **15th First-Frost** (S02): At the lists. Did not turn his head to the south end during Cassian's bout. Did not speak in the box during the third hour. At the closing wine, drank instead of speaking; was crossed to by Cassian and given the proper greeting in the open.
- **16th First-Frost** (S02): The family parlour. The under-shield concession. Spoke to Cassian on the field. The clap on the shoulder, held for a count of two.
- **17th–22nd First-Frost** (S02): With Eira at his side at the lists, at the long table at the family dinners, at the small private chapel on the 20th when the Queen invited the family to a brief mid-tourney rite. Hawking field on the 21st with Sera and Eira.
- **23rd First-Frost** (S02): Closing feast. Stood for his sister at the dais. Lifted the cup.

## Knowledge
- Affairs of state at heir-level. Less than the King but more than any other royal.
- Battlefield ice magic at advanced level.
- About Sera's magic stopping at party tricks. Has wondered, occasionally, whether it is connected to the thing-that-happened. Has not asked her.
- About Aerwin Vornlin: knows what was presented at the pavilion on the 14th; knows what his father has been doing in private audience with Vornlin since. Has not been brought into the question yet, by his father's choice, which Theodric reads correctly.
- *Does not know* that his mother knows about him and Caelum. May suspect — by the closing days, watching his mother watch him, he may suspect she has, in fact, known longer than he has himself been with Caelum.

## At the gate at first light
Will be there. Will give Sera the public Crown Prince's farewell and, in private, before she mounts, the older-brother one. Has something to say to her in private that he has not yet said aloud, possibly about Caelum, possibly about Eira, possibly about himself.
