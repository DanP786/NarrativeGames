# Crown Prince Theodric Caldwyn

## Status
Alive. 25. Crown Prince of Caldros, heir to the Rime Throne. Married three years to Princess Eira née Aelthyn. No children yet — a fact the court has begun to notice and the Queen has begun to ask about, gently.

## Description
The textbook Caldwyn heir. Tall like his father, broader through the shoulders, the same long face but warmer in expression — Theodric has his mother's mouth. Black hair cut to the jaw. Silver-steel eyes. Carries himself with the easy confidence of a man who has been told, since he could walk, that the realm was already his.

A genuinely good knight, a genuinely good rider, a competent battlefield commander. Has the family ice magic at proper royal strength — not the land-binding (that comes only at coronation), but the working magic of a powerful Caldwyn lord. Can frost a yard of ground in winter; can drop the temperature in a chamber by ten degrees if he is angry; cracked a doorframe at fifteen, casually, by leaning on it during an argument with his father.

## Voice & mannerisms
Easy, warm, slightly self-deprecating in private; formal in public, where he has been trained for thirty years. Laughs readily and well. Listens more than he speaks. The court generally loves him. The court generally trusts him.

The cost of being him shows in small places. He drinks more than he used to. He sleeps badly. The household has noticed the lights in his study at the second watch.

## Goals & pressures
- Be the heir his father needs him to be. (He is. Effortlessly. This is the part of his life that is not a problem.)
- Be the husband his wife deserves. (He is trying. He is failing in a specific direction.)
- Be the man Caelum loves. (He is. This is the part of his life that is not a problem and is also the problem.)
- Sire an heir. The pressure on this is mounting and is the conversation he most does not want to have.

## Relationship to Sera
**Loyal.** Older-brother fond. He has loved her since she was small; she has, by being who she is, become one of the two people in his life with whom he does not have to pretend.

Sera figured out about him and Caelum before either of them did. She has held the secret without ever asking for the favor that holding a secret like this normally implies. He has never quite known how to thank her for that, so he has done so in small ways — letting her see him as he is, when no one else is in the room. The three of them — Theodric, Caelum, Sera — are a triangle whose other vertices know how much weight the third is carrying.

## Relationship to Eira
Correct. Public. Kind in a way that is more about manners than about love, and Eira has begun to notice the difference. He has not slept with her in some months. Whether she has noticed, whether she has decided not to notice, whether she will eventually demand to be noticed — open.

## History with Sera
- Older brother since her birth. Doted on her as a small child.
- Was at home when the thing happened that Sera was eleven or twelve for; he was sixteen, present, did something or did not. The two of them have never spoken about it directly. He thinks about it more than he says.
- Welcomed Caelum into his household as a sworn man-at-arms a year ago, when Caelum was knighted at twenty — at which point Sera, then nineteen, already knew Caelum better than Theodric did.
- The affair began three years ago, around the time of Theodric's marriage. He was twenty-two, Caelum was eighteen. Sera was seventeen. She knew within a season.
- **First-Frost 14th, 1142** (S01): Down at the competitors' end for most of the morning; returned to the pavilion at midday and took his chair beside Eira, gave her the small public-courtesy kiss at the temple. Did not look at Sera through the afternoon. **At the ninth bell, retired to his own apartments — with Eira — half a bell before Sera came looking for Caelum. This is not his pattern.** Whether his mother spoke to him at any point during the day is unknown to Sera.

## Knowledge
- Affairs of state at heir-level. Less than the King but more than any other royal.
- Battlefield ice magic at advanced level.
- About Sera's magic stopping at party tricks. Has wondered, occasionally, whether it is connected to the thing-that-happened. Has not asked her.
- Knows nothing he should not know about Ostvar, Therond, Aerinhal beyond what the council has told him. He is not a schemer; if his father has plans, he has not yet shared the depths.
