# Ser Caelum Vesper

## Status
Alive. 21. Second son of Lord Vesper, a minor Caldros lord whose seat lies a day's ride east of Caldemere. Knighted a year ago, at twenty. Sworn man-at-arms to Crown Prince Theodric's personal household. **The Crown Prince's lover for the last three years.** Princess Seraphina's best friend for twelve. **As of the closing days of the Hoarfrost Tourney, also working master of arms to the royal house** — the formal title bestowed by Aelric at the start of the tourney, partly to give the Crown Prince's lover a public role the court could read straight, partly because Caelum is in fact good at the work.

## Description
Tall, slim, sandy-brown hair worn just long enough to fall in his eyes when he is tired. Hazel eyes. Slight freckles across the bridge of his nose, which he hated at fifteen and has made peace with. The kind of face that is more handsome on second look than on first — the smile rearranges the rest of it.

Has a small magical aptitude (Vesper blood is thin for ice; he was never expected to be a battlefield mage). His working capacity is roughly *Sera's nominal ceiling pushed slightly higher* — a real chill to a blade edge, a personal field around himself in winter, modest. Has never minded that he is not powerful.

## Voice & mannerisms
Warm, ready, the kind of voice you trust before you have thought about whether you should. Quick to smile. Quicker than he should be to take a teasing jab — he gives as good as he gets, and Sera and Daron have both been on the losing end of his tongue once or twice.

In private, with Theodric or Sera or both, he is quieter and more careful, and the careful is what tells you what the affair has cost him.

**He has a small dry sound** — a half-cough, half-throat-clear — that he has been making in Sera's vicinity since they were eleven and twelve, when one of them wished to signal to the other *I have seen and I am choosing not to be in this room.* Sera, in fifteen years, has never heard him use it for anyone else.

## Goals & pressures
- Love Theodric without destroying him.
- Be a good knight in a household he has sworn to.
- Keep Sera laughing on the days she most does not want to. *(Resolved across the closing days of the tourney — she is leaving in a state he can live with. He has done the work.)*
- Survive his own life.
- **As of the closing feast: keep his public face through the season Sera is gone.** With Lysandra now visibly his ally and Theodric beginning to mend with Eira, his position has shifted from acute to chronic. He no longer expects to die badly this winter. He does not yet expect to live well, either.

## Relationship to Sera
**Devoted.** Arrived at Caldemere at nine, fostered into court service. Sera was eight. They were trouble together within a month. The friendship is older than every other relationship in his adult life.

**Across the tourney:**

- **14th First-Frost** (S01): Saw Sera against the wall under Cassian's cloak in the corridor; cleared his throat once and walked past. Did not stop at her door. Slept badly.
- **15th First-Frost morning, the king's withdrawing of the royal pavilion** (S02): Sera pulled him into the curtained retiring room. The conversation that put the foundation under everything else of the session. **Memorable lines (selected):**
  - *"I have been waiting for you to come and find me since the ninth bell last night."*
  - *"You did not betray me. Your mother has known three years."*
  - *"You are fifteen years old. — I misspoke. You are nineteen."* (His memory-tell; she is in fact twenty. He has been catching himself on her age since she was thirteen.)
  - *"That you finally said the wrong thing to the one person who already knew is not a betrayal. It is the cost of asking a [twenty]-year-old to be older than her years for four years."*
  - *(on the corridor)* *"I will not ask you anything you do not want to tell me. Was it a thing being done to you, or a thing you were choosing?"*
  - First hug since their grandfather's funeral four winters ago.
  - **The three points he gave her:**
    1. *Your mother will not move on Theodric in the next nine days. Theo is safe through the closing feast at least. Eira is safe through then. We have time.*
    2. *The Aelthyn letter is good work, Sera. Better than you know. — I am proud of you.*
    3. *If, when you sit with it tonight before the wine, you find that you can imagine waking in twenty years and being glad it was him — then accept, and do it gladly, and do not let the fact that your mother chose him first take a single morning of that gladness from you.*
- **15th First-Frost evening** (S02): Cleared the night-woman from the alcove of Sera's corridor before the ninth bell. Did this without being asked. The night-woman was sent on the working pretext of an errand that the night-woman has the discretion not to find suspicious; Caelum's quiet kindness to his master-of-arms register, a thing neither he nor Sera have spoken about since.
- **15th First-Frost on the third hour at the lists** (S02): One hand white on the rail through Cassian's second pass. Did not turn his head when Marrick Ardenmark aimed under the shield.
- **16th First-Frost upper gallery, after the audience chamber** (S02): Private exchange with Sera. *"He told me about the orchard. I told him about the year you were thirteen. He has my face."* — meaning: Cassian and Caelum had a private conversation, on Caelum's initiative, after the audience chamber concluded; Caelum let Cassian see a piece of him; Cassian let Caelum see a piece in return; *he has my face* meaning *I have decided he is one of mine.* Sera understood. Did not press for details.
- **17th–21st First-Frost** (S02): Public face. At the rail. At the door of every chamber Sera went into for a private session — Lysandra's, Aelric's, Theodric's. Without being asked.
- **22nd First-Frost evening** (S02): At Sera's chamber door late, after the Queen had left the small parlour. Their second embrace since the grandfather's funeral, longer than the first. **Memorable line:** *"Sera-min. — Go well."* Nothing more. Walked away in working steel down the corridor without turning at the corner.
- **23rd First-Frost** (S02): The closing feast. At the rail of the dais in working steel as master of arms. **Hand on the wood gone white during the announcement.** Allowed himself one direct look at Sera under the cheer — *Sera-min. Sera-min.* — and looked away.

## Relationship to Theodric
The affair has been three years long. It began when he was eighteen and Theodric was twenty-two, around the time of Theodric's marriage to Eira.

**Across the tourney:** has not been seen alone with Theodric since the night of the 14th, by his own discipline and Theodric's. They have spoken three times in the week, all in working contexts where the household could see them. **Whether Theodric has come to him in private at the bachelors' apartments is not known to Sera; if he has, it has been brief and quiet, and the household has not registered.** Eira's apartments have had the most of Theodric this week.

The affair is not resolved. It has been suspended. Caelum has, by silent compact with Lysandra and Sera, accepted that the question will be held over until after Sera's wedding at the earliest. He does not know what is on the far side of the Feast of the Lesser Lights.

## Relationship to Lysandra
**Newly explicit conspirator.** Has known three years that she has known three years. Has had two short conversations with the Queen this week — one in her sitting room on the evening of the 16th (~quarter of an hour, behind closed doors, content unknown to Sera) and one in the herb-garden at the eighth bell on the morning of the 22nd (a working circuit, three minutes, public). The Queen has, by her own hand, made him master of arms to the royal house at the start of the tourney; the position came with a household stipend and a private chamber in the inner wing, both of which are now Caelum's permanently regardless of what happens to the affair. He has not yet decided whether this is a kindness or a tether. He suspects both.

## History with Sera
- Arrived at Caldemere at nine; Sera was eight. They have been a unit since.
- Was present for the thing that happened when Sera was eleven or twelve. He was twelve. He remembers most of it. They have spoken about it twice — once when Sera was fourteen, once when she was nineteen. The first conversation went badly. The second went better. Neither has resolved it.
- Watched Sera turn into the woman the court calls the Ice Princess and has his own opinion about which parts of that are real.
- **14th–23rd First-Frost** (S01–S02): see above.

## Knowledge
- About the affair with Theodric — everything from his side.
- About Sera's interior: more than anyone else, less than he thinks.
- About the thing that happened when Sera was eleven or twelve: most of the surface. Some of the depth. Not all of it.
- About Daron's underside-of-Caldemere life: a surprising amount. He does not flatter Daron by mentioning it.
- About affairs of state: limited. Now slightly less limited — master-of-arms position gives him room access to small councils he had not been at before.
- **About Cassian Verrent:** has had a private conversation with him on the morning of the 16th after the audience chamber. Has, by his own private reckoning, decided that Cassian is *one of his.* Will hold for the years.
- *Knows now* that Lysandra has known three years. Sera told him so on the morning of the 15th, in the king's withdrawing of the royal pavilion.

## At the gate at first light
Will be there. Will hold the protocol position of master of arms to the royal house — at the King's left elbow, three paces back. Will not approach Sera personally; that goodbye has been said at her chamber door on the 22nd. **Will, however, almost certainly, before the gate opens, make the small dry throat-clear at the back of the courtyard so Sera hears it and knows.** That is all. He will not look at her once she is past him.
