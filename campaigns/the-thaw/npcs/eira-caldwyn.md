# Crown Princess Eira Caldwyn née Aelthyn

## Status
Alive. 23. Crown Princess of Caldros. Born to House Aelthyn of the Frostmarch — the great fortress-house of Caldros's northern border, vassals to the crown for two hundred years, married into the royal line three years ago to consolidate the bond between Caldemere and the Frostmarch.

**As of the closing days of the Hoarfrost Tourney, sitting close to her husband again in a way the household has not seen in three years. Something between her and Theodric shifted on the night of 14th First-Frost.**

## Description
Tall for a woman, fair-haired the colour of pale wheat, blue-grey eyes. Pretty in the strong, well-made way northern Caldros women often are. Carries herself with the upright stillness of a woman who was raised in a fortress and never quite left it, even when the fortress was a different building.

Has the full ice magic of a powerful Caldwyn-grade noble — Aelthyn blood is dense with the gift; that is why the marriage was made. She can summon mist, layer frost across a room, ice a stretch of standing water at twenty paces. Properly trained, well-disciplined, slightly stronger than Theodric in raw working magic though not in finesse.

## Voice & mannerisms
Quiet, precise, slightly formal even at home. Listens carefully; answers carefully. Her humor is dry and shows up only with people she trusts — a circle of perhaps four. **As of the Hoarfrost Tourney that circle has acquired a fifth: Sera.** The Frostmarch *e* in her vowels comes forward when the curtain is down, and so does, occasionally, a small involuntary blush. The blue-grey eyes do not blink away from a question, even a hard one. The Frostmarch does not, apparently, do indirection when the curtain is down.

## Goals & pressures
- Be the Crown Princess Caldros expects. (She is.)
- Be a mother. (She wants to be. The not-yet has begun to ache.)
- Be loved by her husband. (She thought, at twenty, that this was secondary. She is twenty-three now and has revised. **As of the closing days of the tourney she is, with reservations, daring to hope.**)
- Hold the bond between Aelthyn and the crown. The Frostmarch's loyalty runs through her.
- **Have a friend in Caldemere who is not assigned to her.** Set up by Sera; Riannon Aelthyn (Eira's cousin, of Otrim's three sisters, 22, yard-trained until twelve, has been writing Eira long monthly letters from the Frostmarch for three years) accepted the invitation by return courier on the 21st and will travel south to Caldemere within the month. Riannon will not arrive before Sera leaves.

## Relationship to Sera
**Friendly → Loyal.** Opened on the afternoon of the 14th. Hardened across the nine days of the tourney. The two women have, by the close of session 2, become friends in fact and not only by intention.

**Across the tourney:**
- **15th First-Frost.** The Aelthyn letter taken to Eira at the third bell by Lysandra and Sera together, in Eira's own apartments. Eira signed it under the Queen's hand without hesitation. *"Riannon. — Yes. I should have asked for her myself two years ago and did not know I was allowed to."*
- **15th First-Frost evening.** Sat at Theodric's side at the closing wine; said nothing publicly; squeezed Sera's hand once when Cassian crossed the box.
- **16th First-Frost.** Family parlour breakfast. Sitting closer to Theodric than the morning before. *"Theodric. I should like to come with you when you do."* When Theodric said yes, the small involuntary blush.
- **16th First-Frost late morning.** Stood at Theodric's shoulder on the field while he spoke to Cassian. Did not speak. Hand once briefly on Theodric's elbow.
- **17th–20th First-Frost.** At Theodric's side at the lists every afternoon. At the long table at the family dinners.
- **20th First-Frost.** The small private chapel mid-tourney rite the Queen called. Eira stood between Theodric and Sera; held Sera's wrist for a count of three at the end of the prayer.
- **21st First-Frost.** The hawking field with Theodric and Sera. Cold clear weather. Eira flew her grandmother's small Aelthyn hawk (a thing she had not done since coming south; the hawk lives at Caldemere mews and has been kept gentle for her by the master of mews for three years against the day). At the hunting cottage, with Theodric a few paces off seeing to the horses, Eira said quietly to Sera: *"I will miss you, Sera. — I had not realised, when this all began, that I had not yet started missing you."*
- **22nd First-Frost.** A private call to Sera's chambers in the afternoon, brief, formal in dress but not in face. Gave Sera a small folded scrap of paper with three Aelthyn-house phrases of farewell in the old Frostmarch tongue, written in her own hand — *"in case you find yourself wanting to say goodbye in a language Aerinhal does not know."*
- **23rd First-Frost.** Closing feast. Pale green with the small Aelthyn pearl. At Theodric's side on the dais, sitting close. Public smile that was not public when the announcement was spoken. Stood with her cup at her chest during the cheer.

## Relationship to Theodric
**Mending.** **On the night of 14th First-Frost, Theodric retired to his own apartments with Eira at the ninth bell** — not his pattern. *What happened in those apartments has not been told to anyone, including Sera, by either of them.* Eira's eyelids on the morning of the 16th were faintly raw. By the closing days of the tourney they were sitting close; by the closing feast they were a married couple at the dais again, in something more than form.

What has changed:
- Theodric has come to her chamber three of the eight nights since the 14th. (Not all eight; this is not yet a recovered marriage. But three.)
- Theodric speaks to her in private now. The night of the 14th was, at minimum, a long conversation.
- Eira has not asked Theodric for the truth about Caelum. *She may already know it, by her own observation, and have decided to take her husband as he comes home rather than as he might have been. Or she may not know and is choosing not to see.* This is the largest piece of opaque country on the campaign map.

## History with Sera
- Met formally before the wedding, three years ago. Sera was seventeen, Eira was twenty.
- Has sat at family dinners with Sera perhaps two hundred times. Until 14th First-Frost — three real conversations.
- **14th First-Frost** (S01): Pavilion afternoon. Sera crossed two seats; cup of warmed wine; the Frostmarch yards in her own voice; *"are you lonely?"*; *"yes."*
- **15th–23rd First-Frost** (S02): see above.

## Knowledge
- Caldros affairs of state at Crown Princess level.
- Battlefield ice magic at advanced level. Long-knife and short-spear at lapsed-Frostmarch-yard level.
- The Frostmarch and its politics inside out — that is her childhood.
- About Sera's magic stopping at party tricks: knows the fact; has been too polite to ask about it. *(Has, in the closing days, mentioned offhandedly to Sera that her own Aelthyn grandmother could not work the gift at all until she was forty-three. The remark was not, Sera judged, accidental.)*
- About Theodric and Caelum: **open question — see above.** May already know. May be choosing not to know. May truly not know.

## At the gate at first light
Will be there. Will embrace Sera publicly in the Aelthyn style — both hands on Sera's upper arms, foreheads briefly touched, three Frostmarch words spoken low. The court will read it as Frostmarch warmth; Sera will read it as the most-formal-she-has-ever-been with her sister-in-law, deliberately, because the alternative is to cry where the foreign delegations can see.
