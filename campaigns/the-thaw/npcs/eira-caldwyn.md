# Crown Princess Eira Caldwyn née Aelthyn

## Status
Alive. 22. Crown Princess of Caldros. Born to House Aelthyn of the Frostmarch — the great fortress-house of Caldros's northern border, vassals to the crown for two hundred years, married into the royal line three years ago to consolidate the bond between Caldemere and the Frostmarch.

## Description
Tall for a woman, fair-haired the colour of pale wheat, blue-grey eyes. Pretty in the strong, well-made way northern Caldros women often are. Carries herself with the upright stillness of a woman who was raised in a fortress and never quite left it, even when the fortress was a different building.

Has the full ice magic of a powerful Caldwyn-grade noble — Aelthyn blood is dense with the gift; that is why the marriage was made. She can summon mist, layer frost across a room, ice a stretch of standing water at twenty paces. Properly trained, well-disciplined, slightly stronger than Theodric in raw working magic though not in finesse.

## Voice & mannerisms
Quiet, precise, slightly formal even at home. Listens carefully; answers carefully. Her humor is dry and shows up only with people she trusts — a circle of perhaps four. **Until First-Frost 14th** had not shown her feelings in public. Has been trained out of it. — The Frostmarch *e* in her vowels comes forward when the curtain is down, and so does, occasionally, a small involuntary blush; Sera has now seen both. The blue-grey eyes do not blink away from a question, even a hard one. **The Frostmarch does not, apparently, do indirection when the curtain is down.**

## Goals & pressures
- Be the Crown Princess Caldros expects. (She is.)
- Be a mother. (She wants to be. The not-yet has begun to ache.)
- Be loved by her husband. (She thought, at twenty, that this was secondary. She is twenty-two now and has revised.)
- Hold the bond between Aelthyn and the crown. The Frostmarch's loyalty runs through her.
- **Have a friend in Caldemere who is not assigned to her.** New, as of First-Frost 14th.

## Relationship to Sera
**Friendly (opened).** Until First-Frost 14th this was *correct and distant.* On the afternoon of that day Sera crossed two seats in the pavilion to sit with her — the first time the two women had sat alone together in three years of marriage. They exchanged a cup of warmed wine. Eira blushed (a girl's blush, not a Crown Princess's). They talked about Otrim's footwork, then about Otrim, then about the Frostmarch yards, then about Eira herself. Sera asked plainly: *are you lonely?* Eira answered, plainly: *yes.* They have agreed in everything but speech to know each other now.

Eira does not yet know what Sera knows about Theodric. She has *not* yet asked Sera what was the matter Sera was being gentle with her about — has decided that not asking is its own dignity. She has begun, in the last few months, to wonder whether her husband loves her. She has not, until First-Frost 14th, had anyone in Caldemere she could ask.

## Relationship to Theodric
Correct, formal, beginning to be lonely. She knew at the wedding that he did not love her the way the songs would have suggested. She accepted this; love-marriages are for stories. She did not expect to mind so much three years on.

**On the night of First-Frost 14th, Theodric retired to his own apartments with Eira at the ninth bell.** This is not his pattern. Eira has not, by sundown, communicated to Sera or anyone what this meant. What happened in his apartments that night is, for now, opaque.

## History with Sera
- Met formally before the wedding, three years ago. Sera was seventeen, Eira was nineteen.
- Has sat at family dinners with Sera perhaps two hundred times since.
- Three real conversations before First-Frost 14th — none of which had crossed into the personal.
- **First-Frost 14th, 1142** (S01):
  - Pavilion afternoon. Sera crossed two seats and sat with her. Eira: *"You have not sat with me before. I have wondered if I should have asked you to."*
  - Cup of warmed wine, blushed.
  - Discussed Otrim's bout (his footwork; *"he is the slowest-looking large man you have ever seen"*).
  - Admitted to Frostmarch yard-training in long-knife and short-spear until twelve. Admitted she misses it. *"He is the first man whose feet I learned to watch."*
  - Plainly asked Sera: *"Why did you come to sit with me, Sera?"*
  - Heard Sera's word *cold-hearted* and rejected it: *"Cold-hearted women do not do different things with their hands."*
  - Said plainly: *I am lonely.* And: *I do not say it to Theodric.*
- Tomorrow morning: will be asked (probably by Sera, possibly by the Queen via Sera) which of Otrim's three sisters she would like summoned north from the Frostmarch.

## Knowledge
- Caldros affairs of state at Crown Princess level.
- Battlefield ice magic at advanced level. Long-knife and short-spear at lapsed-Frostmarch-yard level.
- The Frostmarch and its politics inside out — that is her childhood.
- About Sera's magic stopping at party tricks: knows the fact; has been too polite to ask about it.
- About Theodric and Caelum: *does not know.* **May be moving toward suspicion.** What she knows is that her husband is no longer coming to her chamber, and she has begun to ask herself why.
