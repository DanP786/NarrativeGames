# King Aelric Caldwyn

## Status
Alive. 54. King of Caldros, holder of the Rime Throne for thirty-one years. In late middle age. Health described as "good for a king who has held the Pact this long" — which is to say, beginning to fail in small, private ways he does not advertise.

## Description
Tall, lean, the long Caldwyn face. Hair iron-grey now, beard kept short and trimmed close. Eyes the same silver-steel as his daughter's. Carries himself with the quiet that comes from having been the most powerful man in a room for three decades. The room generally agrees.

**At the closing feast of the Hoarfrost Tourney he wore the great crown for the first time since the spring after the war.** This was a deliberate signal to the foreign delegations and the Lower City both.

## Voice & mannerisms
Low, dry, sparing with words. Rarely raises his voice; does not need to. When he is amused his face barely changes; those who know him watch for a small tightening at the corner of the mouth. Pauses before answering, sometimes for a long time. Most men cannot sit through the pause.

In private, with his daughter, his voice goes one register lower. He uses the childhood name *Sera-min* in rooms where the court cannot hear him.

## Goals & pressures
- Hold the realm through the rest of his reign without a war.
- See Theodric married, sired, and ready. (Married is done. The other two are not — though Eira and Theodric have begun, in the closing days of the tourney, to look like a marriage again.)
- **See Sera safely south.** Made the decision personally; took responsibility for it; gave Sera his real reason. The condition (month at the Verrent house, Aerinhal-rite wedding at the Feast of the Lesser Lights, residency south thereafter) is his.
- Manage the Ostvar succession question. He has not yet tipped his hand on Aerwin Vornlin; he is holding.
- Stay alive long enough to be the one who chooses when to step away from the Pact.

## Relationship to Sera
**Devoted.** Has loved her his whole life; has, in the audience chamber on the 16th, made the decision a father has to make once and named it without flinching.

**Memorable lines from the audience chamber:**
- *"I am going to accept the proposal, Sera."*
- *"You will be betrothed to the Prince of Aerinhal at the closing feast of this tourney. The betrothal will be announced from the dais by the Aerinhal Ambassador, with my consent given in the Caldros tongue and Cassian's father's consent given by letter in the Aerinhal."*
- *"At the close of the tourney, in nine days, you will travel south with the Aerinhal delegation. You will reside in your future father-in-law's house for one month from the day of arrival. That month will be your engagement. At the end of that month you will be married in the Aerinhal cathedral by Aerinhal rite, with such Caldros rites added as my Lord Chamberlain and your mother shall arrange. You will reside in Aerinhal as the wife of Prince Cassian Verrent."*
- *"I am not punishing you, Sera. You are not being sent away. You are being placed where the match will, in my judgement, take."*
- *"I do not want to lose you a fourth time, Sera-min. Three is enough."*
- *(at parting)* *"The cloak is well cut. — Tell the prince, when you next see him, that I noticed it."*

He granted Sera's four requests in the audience without hesitating on any of them: Anwen south; a day in the orchard with Daron; a ride with Theodric and Eira; an evening with her mother.

At the closing feast he spoke the consent himself, from the dais, without writing a reply. Under the cheer, looking at his daughter, he said *"my child. My son."*

## History with Sera
- Father since birth. Doted on her as a small child.
- The thing that happened when Sera was eleven or twelve — the family does not discuss it — the King knows about. He did something afterward, or did not, that has shaped his relationship with her since. *(Still unspoken.)*
- Backed her choice-of-husband privilege at sixteen against the Queen's mild objection.
- **14th First-Frost** (S01): Opened and closed the tourney from the pavilion. Was visibly still when the third Ostvar envoy presented Aerwin Vornlin's claim — the Rime Court had not previously acknowledged that claimant. His closing invocation included: *"And let the realm see who has come."* Did not approach Sera or Lysandra in the public part of the day.
- **15th First-Frost** (S02): Received the Aerinhal proposal letter on his desk at the eighth bell of the 16th, having known by the night before that Yarrick would have made the approach. *(Actually — letter was on his desk by first light on the 16th; he had been told by Lysandra at first light by note that Yarrick had said yes the night before.)*
- **16th First-Frost** (S02): The audience chamber. See above. Did not pull his daughter close in the formal chamber; held her hand instead, large, dry, warm, for one breath. Released her himself.
- **17th–22nd First-Frost** (S02): Took the Aerinhal delegation in formal audience at the third hour on the 16th to ratify; received them in his private writing-chamber on the 17th and 19th for working sessions on the marriage contract. Held two private councils on the Vornlin question.
- **23rd First-Frost** (S02): The closing feast. Wore the great crown. Spoke the consent from the dais himself. Under the cheer: *"My child. My son."*

## Knowledge
- Everything a king of his standing knows. Treaties, balances, who is paying whom.
- The full mechanism of the Hoarfrost Pact.
- About Sera's magic stopping at party tricks. Has theories he has not shared. Will not share them this campaign without an opening.
- **About Theodric and Caelum:** *open question.* If anyone in the older generation suspects, it is him. The Queen has decided not to put the information into his hands; whether his own observation has put it into his hands separately is unknown.
- The marriage contract for Sera in detail — Lord Chamberlain has the draft; both signatures will be on it before the wedding. Some clauses are not yet on paper.
- About Aerwin Vornlin's claim: the file. Has made no public statement. Will hold through the Frost.

## At the gate at first light
Will be there. Will speak only the small formal words to the Aerinhal delegation in front of the court. The private words have been said; the audience chamber on the 16th was the audience he means to live with.
