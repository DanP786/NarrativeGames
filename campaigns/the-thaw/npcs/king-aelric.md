# King Aelric Caldwyn

## Status
Alive. 54. King of Caldros, holder of the Rime Throne for thirty-one years. In late middle age. Health described as "good for a king who has held the Pact this long" — which is to say, beginning to fail in small, private ways he does not advertise.

## Description
Tall, lean, the long Caldwyn face. Hair iron-grey now, beard kept short and trimmed close. Eyes the same silver-steel as his daughter's. Carries himself with the quiet that comes from having been the most powerful man in a room for three decades. The room generally agrees.

## Voice & mannerisms
Low, dry, sparing with words. Rarely raises his voice; does not need to. When he is amused his face barely changes; those who know him watch for a small tightening at the corner of the mouth. Pauses before answering, sometimes for a long time. Most men cannot sit through the pause.

## Goals & pressures
- Hold the realm through the rest of his reign without a war.
- See Theodric married, sired, and ready. (Married is done. The other two are not.)
- See his daughter settled in a life she can carry — *which is not the same as the life the Queen wants for her, and he knows it.*
- Manage the Ostvar succession question. He has not yet tipped his hand.
- Stay alive long enough to be the one who chooses when to step away from the Pact.

## Relationship to Sera
**Devoted, with limits.** He loves her, indulges her where he can, and has so far backed her right to choose her own husband. The "with limits" is real: he will not back her past the Queen's deadline if the Queen pushes for it, because the marriage of a third-in-line princess is a piece of state, and he is the state. He does not pretend otherwise to himself or to her.

He has, in private moments, given her advice that the Queen would not have given her — about how to wait without being seen to wait, how to read a chamber the Queen reads differently, how to disappoint without offending. He has never told the Queen he does this.

## History with Sera
- Father since birth. Doted on her as a small child.
- The thing that happened when Sera was eleven or twelve — the family does not discuss it — the King knows about. He did something afterward, or did not, that has shaped his relationship with her since.
- Backed her choice-of-husband privilege at sixteen against the Queen's mild objection.
- Has not spoken to her at length in some weeks; he has been preoccupied with Ostvar.
- **First-Frost 14th, 1142** (S01): Opened and closed the tourney from the pavilion. Was visibly still when the third Ostvar envoy presented Aerwin Vornlin's claim — the Rime Court had not previously acknowledged that claimant. His closing invocation included: *"And let the realm see who has come."* Did not approach Sera or Lysandra in the public part of the day.

## Knowledge
- Everything a king of his standing knows. Treaties, balances, who is paying whom.
- The full mechanism of the Hoarfrost Pact.
- About Sera's magic stopping at party tricks. Has theories he has not shared.
- About Theodric and Caelum: *open question.* If anyone in the older generation suspects, it is him.
