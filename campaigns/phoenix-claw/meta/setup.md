# Campaign setup

Created: 10 May 2026 (real-world date)
Slug: phoenix-claw

## Onboarding answers

**Genre / references:** Star Wars, Old Republic era (KOTOR-adjacent — millennia before the films, Sith and Jedi at war, Mandalorian wars in living memory, Republic over-extended).

**Voice:** 3rd person, present tense.

**Length:** Open-ended sandbox.

**Tone:** Full grimdark. Nothing off the table.

**Content posture:** No content limits. Violence, cruelty, sex, trauma, moral despair — all on the table when the fiction calls for it.

**Stakes / scope:** Personal sandbox. She is one bounty hunter in a galaxy at war. Story follows her wherever she takes it.

**Agency posture:** Gritty realism. Failure is frequent and consequential, even at things she's good at, when stakes are high.

**Death rule:** Soft fail only. Lethal-looking outcomes resolve as capture, scarring, lost time, near-death. The world advances without her during the gap.

**Mind-reading rule:** Standard Star Wars. Force-sensitives can sense emotional states (fear, deception, anger); strong ones can push or read surface thoughts with effort. Non-Force-users cannot. Scarlet would feel an attempt as pressure or wrongness, not a violation she can name.

**Main thread:** See `meta/main-thread.md`. *Scarlet wants to see the galaxy. The work is the cost of the freedom. The question: what does it cost her to keep doing this, in a galaxy this dark, while telling herself she's just passing through?*

**Character seed:**
- Scarlet Kriov, 28, human, female, white, blonde shoulder-length straight hair, athletic build slightly under-fed from long hauls.
- Grafted her whole life. Family died in a skirmish she does not know the sides of.
- Saved enough as a mechanic to half-build a freighter. The other half was paid for in years.
- Wants to see the galaxy — different planets, ports, people. Bounty work is the means.
- Takes easy jobs. Just enough credits for fuel, food, port fees, repairs.
- **Brings bounties in alive.** Always. This is the line she draws between herself and what she does.
- An ex-lover taught her to shoot when she said she was going into the work.

**Appearance:**
- Athletic, slim from spacer rations. White, blonde shoulder-length straight hair.
- Tight white vest. Black cargo trousers. Boots. Red crop bomber jacket.
- Two blaster pistols, one on each thigh — stun on one side, lethal on the other.
- Combat knife behind her on the belt.
- Stun carbine kept on the ship; carried into work when the job calls for it.

**Ship:** *Phoenix Claw.* Light freighter / courier-class, ~25–30 metres. Cockpit, kitchenette, single bunk, refresher, holding cell, cargo space. Cramped but functional. Half of it she built herself.

**Opening hook:** Nar Shaddaa, mid-job. Three days into a tail on a low-tier mark — a Twi'lek slicer who skipped on a Hutt's gambling debt. She finds him already dead, and the people who killed him are still in the building.
