# Main thread

## Central question
What does it cost Scarlet to keep doing this work, in a galaxy this dark, while telling herself she is just passing through?

## What she wants
To see the galaxy. Different planets, different ports, different people. Freedom of movement. The horizon, always.

## What's in the way
- The work itself. Bounty hunting in the Old Republic galaxy is not a clean trade. The easy jobs aren't always easy and the line she draws — *alive only* — costs her credits, time, and occasionally skin.
- Her own past. A skirmish she does not understand killed her family. She has not asked who or why. She might, eventually, have to.
- The galaxy's gravity. People who keep moving meet people who want them to stop, or stay, or come work for someone bigger. The longer she works, the harder it gets to keep passing through.

## The line
**She brings them in alive.** This is the spine of the question. The grimdark of the setting will lean on this rule. It is the difference between her and what she does. Whether it holds — and what it costs when it bends or breaks — is what the campaign will quietly be about.

## Status
**active — first major test passed, the question paid out once.** Session 2 hardened the thread on her body. The establishing arc, hardened in S1, was tested in S2 against the people who had paid her last invoice clean:
- **Antagonist surfaced fully.** *The spiked spiral is a major political-shadow faction.* The party that bought the spring Coruscant trade-vote, owns the Aargau private vault, runs the Mortuda safehouse system, contracts through the Phantom Voice broker, and maintains a working "list of names" — the same faction that paid Scarlet 20k for Avash now paid her 35k for Pyx's death. Scarlet has worked for them twice. Vel is their in-person handler at Worlport. Distinct from and at war with the **Vesper** (the plain-coat killers from S1).
- **The line was tested and held.** *Alive, always.* When her own employer wanted the man dead, Scarlet renegotiated the contract face-to-face: traded the box and the dossier (her better leverage) for the bounty and the man's life and parole. **First job in which her line survived contact with the people who paid for the bringing-in.**
- **The cost was named.** *Eighteen months of someone else's grief on her ship. Risa's bracelet handed back to the people who killed her, without knowing. The intimate register pulled back to the off-world drop. Better leverage liquidated for one man's life. The faction is now sharper about who she is, and not in her hand any more.*
- **Theron is on the ship.** Operating under his real name from Bonadan onward, the *Pyx* run ended. Whether he is a thread that comes back, or a closed page that stays closed, is open.

The campaign continues. The thread updates reactively as Scarlet keeps moving. **Open-ended; no planned endpoint.**
