# Session 03 — 13 May 2026 (real-world)

## Scene 1 — *Phoenix Claw*, Kel Sahr → Bonadan, the first night of transit

Day 1 of the five-day vector. Scarlet locks the Bonadan autopilot, rotates her chair, and asks Theron what they should do to pass the time. He reads the seam in the question — *"that look is doing more work than the question"* — and hands the ball back. She rises, undresses on the walk to the bunk (jacket on the co-pilot chair, vest at the cockpit doorway, the rest at the kitchenette and the cabin floor), and pulls him through the cabin frame by the front of his coat after he has waited at the threshold for her to call him.

He follows by his own explicit hand-off — *"I am out of practice. You will have to lead"* — and keeps his word the whole way down: hands under the mattress edge, not on her head, not directing, until she works back up to him and he flips her under him on the count of his own readiness. *"My turn."* He stays with her rhythm until hers is done before he takes his own. *"The longest breath he has let out tonight."* Her name in it. Past keeping. Her first speaking of his — *"Theron"* — comes during the act; the first time she has said it aloud since he gave it to her on the suborbital. He answers with hers. *"The way you answer."*

In the quiet after, faces mirrored — his hand on her cheek, hers on his — Scarlet makes the matching disclosure to his cell-line from Tarvane's Quay: *"I didn't pretend the first night we met. I was attracted to you."* He receives it cleanly. *"I had hoped for the matching half. I was waiting until you told me."* The *"Thank you, Scarlet"* register — closed on the suborbital — is reopened with full weight. *"Not the way he said it on the suborbital. The opposite."*

Then the harder thing. Scarlet, joke-as-disclosure: *"You're not secretly waiting to kill me for ruining your deal and trading your dead girlfriend's bracelet?"* He does not pull back. The dry returns in a different register, then drops entirely:

> *"You did not trade her bracelet. I traded it. You handed me the room and asked the price; I chose what we paid with. The bracelet was mine to pay with. I have been telling myself that for twelve hours, and I think on the bunk just now I finally believed it."*

The first time the trade has been talked through in the open. The bunk has now been named as the place Theron made peace with it. *"Thank you for asking, though. I would rather you ask than wonder."* Then, dry returning *like weather:* *"Also — the deal was not yours to ruin. It was mine to lose. You did not ruin anything."*

They go again — Theron taking the longer time he promised (*"I am going to take longer this time"*), then a Scarlet-led shorter one, then a quiet one past the *Phoenix Claw*'s night-cycle dimming. Scarlet finally admits the bunk pulls out forty centimetres wider; *not enough for two people who do not know each other, plenty for two who do.* Theron falls asleep first, forehead at her collarbone, one arm across her middle — *three years of him not having a person to sleep against. He gives in to it.* Scarlet, last awake, closes her eyes with one of her fingers in the crook of his. The holsters on the shelf do not move. Neither do they.

- Theron, post-coital: *"There is a great deal of time on the run, Scarlet. Most of it is for thinking. Thinking is not the practice. And I did not say I would not improvise."*

### What this scene did
- **The campaign's central question paid out in a new register.** The line — *alive, always* — was tested twelve hours ago against the people who paid for the bringing-in; it has now been tested again against the woman who made the trade. The man she walked out of a room is the man she has now slept with. The trade she made unknowingly has been talked through and lifted off her by his framing. The bunk has now been named as the place that ended both of their carryings.
- **The cell-line loop has been closed by Scarlet aloud.** Theron has the matching half he had been waiting for *"longer than he knew."*
- **The *"Thank you, Scarlet"* register is reset.** The suborbital closing has been overwritten by the bunk opening. *"Not the way he said it on the suborbital. The opposite."*
- **First night of transit became a relationship-defining scene.** Four-and-some days of transit ahead carry a different weight now.
- **Risa's name held out of the room.** Scarlet used *"girlfriend"* in the joke; Theron used *"her"* in the absolution. Neither put the name into the conversation. Mutual care without coordination.
