# Session 04 — 14-15 May 2026 (real-world)

## Scene 1 — *Phoenix Claw*, morning of transit day 2 (Kel Sahr → Bonadan)

The first morning-after. Scarlet woke before her alarm at 06:18 ship-time, slipped out from under Theron's arm, made breakfast for two in the kitchenette in his shirt and her own boots. Eggs, toasted bread and wax cheese, fresh caf. Theron's plate on the heat-strip, hers eaten at the stool. Holsters left on the bunk-shelf all morning.

She took her mug to the cockpit and ran HoloNet queries on Bonadan: CSA jurisdiction with paperwork-light bribes, no Republic-registry overhead for small freighters. The eastern spaceport's third quarter confirmed Theron's description. Three small hunter's-boards visible from the relay; two alive-only she tagged for arrival. **A clean spiked-spiral search returned nothing — no scrubbed warnings even.** The spiral has no visible reach in the Corporate Sector. The query had taken her four searches, none of them clumsy. **Streetwise Adept threshold realised in the chair, alone.**

She washed up and went to the refresher. Theron woke alone in the bunk after eight hours of sleep — **his first eight-hour sleep in three years** — found the breakfast, ate the bread, came to find her. Said her name at the refresher door before stepping in. Hand at her hip not her waist; her hand flat on the pink bracket-scar at his right shoulder.

After the refresher, the bunk-edge. *"How did you sleep?"* / *"Long. And quiet. Three years and some."* / *"And now?"* / *"Now I have had both."* She sat in his lap and combed his hair: *"Sleep as long as you like."* He pressed his forehead through the shirt at her collarbone: *"That is a generous thing to say, Scarlet. I have been in someone's way for three years. The body of the evidence is heavy."* She corrected him: *"You're never in my way."* He: *"I will trust you to tell me when it changes."*

**Risa's name held out of the morning by both.** Mutual care without coordination.

## Scene 3 — Outside Renna's, the trap, the cell, the escape (transit day 3 evening → day 4 pre-dawn)

**The trap closed.** Voice from her left: *"Told you it was your girl in there."* Korro's eyes flicked past her shoulder — he had been waiting for that. Sap-blow at the back of her head. **Korro's boots in her peripheral vision did not move.** Hands at her holsters. The dark.

Berran returned. Cold-pack at her temple from his coat. Repeated the three-sides offer. Then: *"And captain — your friend in the bar… he has not moved from his table because he is being watched."*

Overheard Berran's plan from the common space: *"…the bridge offline by zero-five, she signs at zero-six, we are wheels-up by zero-seven…"* and the female voice (Mirai): *"…he's making caf again…"* Reached the belly access hatch. ## Scene 5 — Theron's return + the fold to Bonadan (transit day 4 → day 6)

Then she said the thing the night had been: *"You didn't come for me."*

Theron answered into the top of her hair, opening register, the honest answer first: *"I know. I want to tell you what I was working through at the table. I will tell you any of it you want to hear, in any order. The other answer — the answer of the man at the table — is that I should have come for you. I do not have a defence of that answer. The rule I gave myself was the wrong rule for the room I was in. I am sorry, Scarlet. I am sorry I was not the man who came for you tonight."*

Scarlet: *"You're right, I shouldn't have expected you to."* She got up: *"Can you launch the flight to Bonadan, I'm going to lie down."* Walked to her bunk.

## Scene 8 — Fold to Tatooine; the cockpit committal; the Naia Tul chit; the handshake (transit days 6→9)

In the cockpit, mid-fold-to-Tatooine. Scarlet stood at the armrests of Theron's chair, face centimetres from his. *"I get why you didn't, he was an ex and we hadn't labelled what we are… or were. But know this — if we're fucking, you're all I'm fucking. Which means I'm yours."*

Theron, no dodge: *"Yes, Scarlet. All of me is yours, in the way you are saying it. There is no other I am with. There has not been one since the suborbital. There will not be one as long as we are in this. If we stop being in it, you tell me; I tell you. We do not let it stop without saying."* He stood, took her hand and at her invitation took the lead. Bunk. The committal worked through in private register — slow, careful, named.

Later, Theron on Korro: *"I understand him, you know… I have been thinking about him for five weeks. Not in a way that is — *with* you. In the way a man thinks about another man whose place at the start of a thing he is at the end of… If you had walked off Bonadan tonight without me — five weeks would have been one count of the road I would have been on… I am not making a defence of him. He paid for the waiting in the wrong currency at the end. That is on him. But I understand the waiting."*

**Three days in fold.** Alternating ship duties and intimacy across the ship — cockpit, kitchenette, corridor, refresher, holding cell (his now-de-facto second cabin), the cabin's wide-side bunk. Theron mapped her carefully; she invited him to lead in private and he took the lead. **He has not yet chosen a private nickname for her. He is waiting for it to land.** Scarlet has registered the wait and not pressed.

**Fold-clock toned the one-hour mark to Tatooine.**

Scarlet brought up the chit at the navicomp. **Naia Tul.** Twi'lek female, late thirties. Freight-line dispute with a junior Hutt called Klornakka. Mos Eisley working-stalls. Alive only, no preference on condition *so long as she has her tongue and her tongue still works.* **The Hutt wants her to answer the question he is going to ask her.** *(The Hutt is going to torture her.)*

Scarlet thought about it. *The captain at twenty-five would not have taken this one.* She had taken it. Said it to the viewport: *"I am going to deliver her. And the Hutt is going to do what the Hutt is going to do… I have done it before. It pays the bay-rent."* Theron silent for a long count, then: *"You have done it before. I have not. I will follow your lead at Mos Eisley. You know how the job is run. I do not."*

Scarlet: *"You don't have to turn bounty hunter just because you're flying with me you know?"* Theron's answer: *"I am not turning bounty hunter… What I can do is the thing next to the thing you do. Stand at the edge of a bar with a drink and watch the door while you work. Open a lock that is in your way. Walk a body to a freighter without it being remarked. I do not need to be holding the chit to be useful on the run."* And: *"The deciding what to take is yours. I am not on the chit."*

Scarlet, grinning: *"Fine, but don't put yourself in harms way. That's my job."* Theron, dry returned: *"That is going to be a difficult promise to keep, Scarlet… I will make you a working trade. I will not put myself in harm's way if you do not put yourself in worse harm than the job asks for. Even-handed."* Held out his hand for the shake.

### What this scene did
- **The relationship explicitly committed and named.** Exclusivity declared and answered. *I'm yours* / *all of me is yours.* The break-clause built in (*you tell me; I tell you*).
- **Theron's role on the *Phoenix Claw* defined.** Not a bounty hunter. The thing next to the thing she does. Asset to her trade, not in it.
- **The handshake on the harm-clause.** Mutual promise — even-handed care.
- **Scarlet took the Naia Tul contract knowing the Hutt's answer-the-question clause.** First contract she has accepted that crosses into the *delivery to torture* register. *The captain at twenty-five would not have taken this one.* Main-thread relevant — Scarlet is paying for the *just passing through* by becoming the kind of captain who takes this kind of job.
