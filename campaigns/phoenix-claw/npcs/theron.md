# Theron (former alias: Pyx)

## Status
Alive. **Aboard the *Phoenix Claw* in fold to Tatooine; second cabin (formerly the holding cell) is now his de-facto room.** Operating under the name **Theron** since Bonadan landing five-plus weeks ago. Committed to Scarlet in private register, mutually declared in the cockpit mid-fold; exclusive on both sides, break-clause built in. Has not yet chosen a private nickname for her; is waiting for one to land. Five hours out of Tatooine atmosphere at session close. **Armed** (his own pistol, restored to its power-pack; Berran's blaster has been with him on Bonadan but the chain-of-custody from Tatooine forward is open). **Off the spiked spiral's list of names** by Vel's parole-by-name, contingent on staying off the boards — a parole he is honouring.

## Description
Mid-30s. Tall. Dark. Sharper-featured than handsome. Scar over the left eyebrow. Three years of running have left him lean and watchful but, in his first 24 hours of freedom, the watchfulness has loosened — shoulders down, stride wider, lets himself touch a sleeve, lets himself sit relaxed on a public shuttle. Two visible older scars on the torso: a pale line across the left ribs, a more recent pink bracket on the right shoulder. Six small smiles given to Scarlet across his first 24 hours of freedom; the fifth was *"what he looks like."*

Walks with weight he does not advertise. Pours caf with both hands. Sits down without fidgeting. The kind of physical economy that comes from a lot of hours in small rooms.

## Voice & mannerisms
- **Register:** dry, slightly archaic, inverted construction. *"That is a piece of the universe I would offer back,"* not *"I'd offer you that part of the galaxy."*
- **Sentences:** short, declarative, occasionally split across two beats with a comma where most speakers would use a period.
- **Names:** uses sparingly and deliberately. The *"Thank you, Scarlet"* register, which he closed with on the suborbital, was reopened on the bunk with full weight; it has now been said with opening register four times.
- **Tells:** quarter-turns a glass when thinking (Tarvane's Quay). Looks at his hands before answering a hard question (the suborbital). His breath catches before his face does; his hands clench under a mattress edge rather than on a partner's body when he is holding back.
- **Comfortable with silence.** Patient by discipline. Does not fill air to fill it. **Honoured an explicit hand-off in intimate territory** — never moved her hands, never moved his own without her invitation, never on her head — until she invited him to take the lead, and even then took it without roughness.
- **Smile:** rare until the Kel Sahr lift. The fifth one of the day was the one that broke past the restraint. *"This one is what he looks like."*

## Traits
- *Spare, archaic register; says less than expected, says it carefully.*
- *Patient by discipline — comfortable with silence, does not fill air.*
- *Owns his choices openly — will lift a weight off another by claiming the trade rather than shifting it ("I traded it. The bracelet was mine to pay with").*
- *Withholds in public; honest in private — the names, the bracelet, the working theory, all surrendered only at his own count.*
- *Reads tells without naming them; honours seams he sees and lets them hold.*

## Short-term goals
- Disembark cleanly at Bonadan eastern spaceport, third quarter, in ~4 days. Take a flat. Register under his own first name. Build a small line of credit inside a week.
- Sleep, eat, recover three years of physiological cost. Sleep somewhere safer than the doors.
- Spend these four days of transit with Scarlet without setting either of them into a corner. The shape of what they are doing is being decided by both, in real time, in a closed space.
- Return Scarlet's hospitality without being a guest she has to manage.

## Long-term goals
- Live under his real name in a galaxy that has held two of his names off the boards for three years. **Bonadan-quiet is the safe move.** Any visible operation pulls him back onto the list.
- (Unspoken to Scarlet, named only as *"a working theory, 18 months unfalsified."*) **Risa's killers.** Whether he ever names the theory aloud, whether he ever moves on it, and what the spiked spiral's parole-by-name costs him if he does, is the open arc question of his future. **The bunk has now been named as the place he made peace with the bracelet trade** — whether the believing is the end of the carrying, or the beginning of something else, is the long question.
- The family name. Held off the boards as long as the first half. Scarlet has it *"the night you ask me a second time."* What he plans to do with the full name if and when it becomes operationally usable again, he has not said.

## Methods & lines
- **Will:** vault-and-escape work, mechanical bypass, slow patience, careful disclosure, dry humour with people he trusts, taking weight off others by owning his own trades, leading or following in intimate territory by explicit hand-off, sleeping in a room he does not control if the person in it has earned the trust, accepting parole conditions in front of armed strangers if the floor is sound.
- **Won't:** publicly operate under any name while on the spiked spiral's list (would be off-parole and dead within a week); pull Scarlet into his own unfinished business uninvited; press her on anything she has not offered (sees the seams, lets them hold); become a guest she has to manage; make the bracelet trade her weight to carry.
- **Last resort:** the working theory about Risa's killers, named aloud — pursuing the bracelet thread. Currently held back, possibly indefinitely. If lethal force became unavoidable, his blaster has been a tool of last resort throughout the Pyx run, not a working trade; he is not trained as a shooter.

## Relationship to Scarlet (public)
**Committed. Exclusive on both sides, mutually declared.**

In fold to Tatooine, in the cockpit, with her hands at the armrests of his chair and her face centimetres from his, Scarlet named the thing they were doing: *"If we're fucking, you're all I'm fucking. Which means I'm yours."* Theron answered without dodging: *"All of me is yours, in the way you are saying it. There is no other I am with. There has not been one since the suborbital. There will not be one as long as we are in this. If we stop being in it, you tell me; I tell you. We do not let it stop without saying."* **Break-clause built in. Risa not named in the room.**

The five weeks of separation on Bonadan that preceded the committal: Theron took a flat, did not sign the lease at the end of the month, did not push the fab-shop interview, kept his comm-frequency at 1-1-4-7-3 on the local relay, did not punch hers, came to the ramp foot on her lift-day at lift-minus-two with his duffel and his hood down, did not assume he could come up. **Asked her plainly: *Are you taking me with you, or are you not.*** Scarlet's answer was an action — picked up the shirt he had set on the ramp foot, said *this is mine,* walked back up the ramp, and turned at the top: *Are you coming?* He came.

**Theron has not yet chosen a private nickname for her.** He is waiting for one to land organically. *Captain* was tried in the cabin during the first round of the committal night and was retired — it is Berran's register, not Theron's.

He owes her his life, the bracelet-trade absolution, the second cabin on her ship, and the answered hospitality. He carries this without making it a leash.
