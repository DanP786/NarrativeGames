# Theron (former alias: Pyx)

## Status
Alive. Aboard the *Phoenix Claw* in transit, day 1 going on day 2 of ~5 to Bonadan eastern spaceport. **Asleep on Scarlet's bunk, entwined, forehead at her collarbone — first time he has slept against another person in three years.** Uncuffed; unarmed (his blaster is in his duffel, no power-pack — that's on the cabin deck-plate with Scarlet's trousers). Formally off the spiked spiral's "list of names" contingent on staying off the boards.

## Description
Mid-30s. Tall. Dark. Sharper-featured than handsome. Scar over the left eyebrow. Three years of running have left him lean and watchful but, in his first hours of freedom, the watchfulness has loosened — shoulders down, stride wider, lets himself touch a sleeve, lets himself sit relaxed on a public shuttle. Two visible older scars on the torso: a pale line across the left ribs, a more recent pink bracket on the right shoulder. Six small smiles given to Scarlet across his first 24 hours of freedom; the fifth was *"what he looks like."*

Walks with weight he does not advertise. Pours caf with both hands. Sits down without fidgeting. The kind of physical economy that comes from a lot of hours in small rooms.

## Voice & mannerisms
- **Register:** dry, slightly archaic, inverted construction. *"That is a piece of the universe I would offer back,"* not *"I'd offer you that part of the galaxy."*
- **Sentences:** short, declarative, occasionally split across two beats with a comma where most speakers would use a period.
- **Names:** uses sparingly and deliberately. The *"Thank you, Scarlet"* register, which he closed with on the suborbital, has been reopened tonight with full weight and has now been said with opening register four times.
- **Tells:** quarter-turns a glass when thinking (Tarvane's Quay). Looks at his hands before answering a hard question (the suborbital). His breath catches before his face does; his hands clench under a mattress edge rather than on a partner's body when he is holding back.
- **Comfortable with silence.** Patient by discipline. Does not fill air to fill it. **Honoured an explicit hand-off in intimate territory — never moved her hands, never moved his own without her invitation, never on her head — until she invited him to take the lead, and even then took it without roughness.**
- **Smile:** rare until tonight. The fifth one of the day was the one that broke past the restraint. *"This one is what he looks like."*

## Goals & pressures
- **Immediate:** disembark at Bonadan, eastern spaceport, third quarter. Take a flat, register under his own name (Theron), set up a small line of credit inside a week. Sleep, presumably, without watching the doors.
- **Wider:** has not said. The Aargau hit was *"not entirely a job"* — Risa's bracelet was the real prize, and the bracelet is now back with the people he believes killed her. Whether Theron will pursue that (and whether the spiked spiral's parole-by-name will hold if he does) is the open question of his future. **He has now told Scarlet that on the bunk he finally believed the trade was his to make.** Whether the believing is the end of the carrying, or the beginning of something else, is open.
- **Constraint:** the list. *"You stay off the list, you stay off the boards. Yes?"* He answered yes in front of Vel. Bonadan-quiet is the safe move. Any visible operation pulls him back onto the list.

## Relationship to Scarlet
**Friendly. Intimate.**

The register that opened at the transit-deck observation strip on Tarvane's Quay was closed by Scarlet on the suborbital approach to Kel Sahr (when she called Risa *girlfriend* rather than the name and pulled back) and **reopened by her at the start of day 1 of the Bonadan transit, in the cockpit and then in her cabin.** Theron followed by his own explicit hand-off — *"You will have to lead"* — and kept his word the whole way down to her: never on her head, never directing, until she worked back up to him and he flipped her on the count of his own readiness. *"My turn."*

In the after, with hands mirrored at each other's faces, Scarlet made the matching disclosure to his cell-line — *"I didn't pretend the first night we met. I was attracted to you."* Theron answered with the words he had been holding for twelve hours and longer: *"I had hoped for the matching half. I was waiting until you told me. Thank you, Scarlet."* The *"Thank you, Scarlet"* register — closed on the suborbital — was reopened with full weight.

Then the harder thing. Scarlet asked, joke-as-disclosure, whether he resented her for the bracelet trade. Theron lifted the trade off her in the open: *"You did not trade her bracelet. I traded it. You handed me the room and asked the price; I chose what we paid with. The bracelet was mine to pay with. I have been telling myself that for twelve hours, and I think on the bunk just now I finally believed it."* The first time the trade has been talked through; the bunk has now been named as the place he made peace with it.

They went a few more rounds — Theron taking the longer time he had promised, then a Scarlet-led shorter one, then a quiet one past the *Phoenix Claw*'s night-cycle dimming — and fell asleep entwined. **He gave in to sleep first.** Scarlet's bunk, it turns out, pulls out forty centimetres wider; *not enough for two people who do not know each other, plenty for two who do.*

**Risa's name has not been spoken aloud in this register.** Both have held it deliberately — *"her"* in his mouth, *"girlfriend"* in hers. Mutual care without coordination.

He owes her his life and now an additional weight off it. He has named both without making them a debt.

Three rounds; the bracelet absolution in the middle of them; the matched cell-line disclosure named in the quiet after; the *"Thank you, Scarlet"* register flipped from closing to opening. Fell asleep first, against her collarbone — first time he has slept against another person in three years.

## Knowledge
- **What he knows about the spiked spiral.** They bought the spring Coruscant trade-vote, owned the Aargau private vault, kept Risa's bracelet there because they could not afford to destroy it, contract through the Phantom Voice broker, maintain a list of names, run the Worlport salt house and Mortuda. He has not named the faction's in-faction name (presumably he does not know it; Vel called it *"us"*).
- **What he knows about Risa's killers.** A working theory, 18 months unfalsified. **He has not named the theory aloud to Scarlet.** The implication of *"The Aargau job was not entirely a job"* is that he believes the spiked spiral (or an adjacent party) was responsible. He has not confirmed.
- **What he knows about Scarlet.** Her name, her ship, her line (*alive, always*), her primary motivation (*"not because I love justice but because I want to see the universe"*), that she has worked for the spiked spiral twice and chose not to deliver him on the second contract, that she carries her professional restraint into intimate territory, that she pulled back when his grief became hers to hold and pulled back open when she had decided to. He has seen her drug a man, walk him through a Bothan concourse on a cover, renegotiate a 35k contract face-to-face, and uncuff a captive in front of armed strangers without losing the floor. **He has now also been on the inside of how she leads.**
- **What he learned tonight.** That the Gold Hour attraction was real on her side too. That the bracelet had been weighing on her for twelve hours. That she trusts him with a hard joke about it. That her bunk pulls out forty centimetres wider than it pretends to. That she will sleep alongside him.
- **What he does NOT know.** Whether Scarlet has any longer-arc relationship with the spiked spiral that he should worry about; whether Cael Soros is going to surface; the contents of Brek'eevo's small-life pile in her kitchenette; her past before the work; the name and circumstances of the ex-lover who taught her to shoot.

## What "Theron" implies (in-fiction, for the campaign)
The first name is enough to register a flat, take small jobs, build a small line of credit. The family name — which he has explicitly told Scarlet is hers *"the night you ask me a second time"* — is presumably tied to a longer paper trail: family of origin, possibly a Republic registration, possibly a court that has a warrant on a younger version of him. He has kept both halves off the boards equally, which means the family name carries weight of its own. **The full name is a campaign-level reveal pending Scarlet's choice.** She has not yet asked.
