# Theron (working alias: Pyx)

## Status
Alive. Aboard the *Phoenix Claw* in transit, day 1 of ~5-6 to Bonadan eastern spaceport. Co-pilot's seat, mug on the console. Uncuffed; unarmed (his blaster is in his duffel, no power-pack — that's in Scarlet's left hip pocket until drop). Formally off the spiked spiral's "list of names" contingent on staying off the boards.

## Description
Mid-30s. Tall. Dark. Sharper-featured than handsome. Scar over the left eyebrow. Three years of running have left him lean and watchful but, in his first hours of freedom, the watchfulness has loosened — shoulders down, stride wider, lets himself touch a sleeve, lets himself sit relaxed on a public shuttle. Two small smiles given to Scarlet across the morning at Kel Sahr; the first ones in the campaign.

Walks with weight he does not advertise. Pours caf with both hands. Sits down without fidgeting. The kind of physical economy that comes from a lot of hours in small rooms.

## Voice & mannerisms
- **Register:** dry, slightly archaic, inverted construction. *"That is a piece of the universe I would offer back,"* not *"I'd offer you that part of the galaxy."*
- **Sentences:** short, declarative, occasionally split across two beats with a comma where most speakers would use a period.
- **Names:** uses sparingly and deliberately. Used Scarlet's name six times across the morning — three times as warmth, twice as acknowledgement, once to close a page.
- **Tells:** quarter-turns a glass when thinking (Tarvane's Quay). Looks at his hands before answering a hard question (the suborbital).
- **Comfortable with silence.** Patient by discipline. Does not fill air to fill it.
- **Smile:** rare. Twice in 24 hours by the time of the Kel Sahr lift. Both small. Both at Scarlet.

## Goals & pressures
- **Immediate:** disembark at Bonadan, eastern spaceport, third quarter. Take a flat, register under his own name (Theron), set up a small line of credit inside a week. Sleep, presumably, without watching the doors.
- **Wider:** has not said. The Aargau hit was *"not entirely a job"* — Risa's bracelet was the real prize, and the bracelet is now back with the people he believes killed her. Whether Theron will pursue that (and whether the spiked spiral's parole-by-name will hold if he does) is the open question of his future. He has not said either way.
- **Constraint:** the list. *"You stay off the list, you stay off the boards. Yes?"* He answered yes in front of Vel. Bonadan-quiet is the safe move. Any visible operation pulls him back onto the list.

## Relationship to Scarlet
**Friendly (closed).** The intimate register that opened at the transit-deck observation strip on Tarvane's Quay (one kiss, mutual sharp read) was carried through the night in the cell, through the salt-house trade, and through the suborbital disclosure cascade, then **closed by Scarlet on the shuttle approach to Kel Sahr** when she called Risa *girlfriend* rather than the name and pulled back. Theron accepted the closure without pushing. He has not maneuvered against it. He is on her ship as a passenger to Bonadan, has restricted himself to the cot and the galley, and has offered to stay in the cot if the galley is too much.

The drink offer (*"somewhere neither of us has worked"*) is withdrawn by both sides. Whether either of them changes their mind across five days of transit is open. He has given her his first name as binding; the family name is hers *"the night you ask me a second time"* — an explicit reopening hinge he has handed her.

He owes her his life. He has named that without making it a debt.

## History with Scarlet
- **Tarvane's Quay, the Gold Hour (~24 hrs ago).** Approached her at the bar after she sat 11 minutes back-to-him in a red dress. *"The boots are giving you away."* Mutual sharp read; name exchange (Pyx / Scarlet); Devaronian bartender comped a second round.
- **Tarvane's Quay, transit-deck observation strip (~23 hrs ago).** Walked her out to the strip with Sothen filling the transparisteel. *"Both of us are allowed one."* Kissed her — slow, hand at jaw, thumb at cheek. Released her when she pulled away. *"That was a working answer."*
- **Tarvane's Quay, the Gold Hour (later that night).** Drank the slipsleep tab she dropped in his second drink. Walked out of the station with her on a drunk-girlfriend cover, knowing what was happening. *"You are going to find out the easy way."*
- ***Phoenix Claw* cell (~20 hrs ago).** Woke 4 hours into transit, cuffed in the holding cell. Confessed the kiss had been real on his side, named himself as *the open* on the Aargau box, placed Avash from the spring trade-vote scandal, concluded *"I do not, yet, know which side of the table I am sitting at."* Watched Scarlet read the spiral-sealed dossier in silence and work out the faction.
- **The plan (~18 hrs ago).** Listened to Scarlet's renegotiation pitch. Named that his off-station buyer could pay more than 35k but could not protect him from the faction's reach. Accepted her plan. Slept.
- **The salt house, Worlport (~6 hrs ago).** Walked in cuffed; uncuffed in front of two operatives; opened the box without looking inside; accepted Vel's parole-by-name with a single *"Yes."* Walked out at Scarlet's shoulder.
- **The walk and the suborbital back (~3 hrs ago).** *"Off-world with you. And then I would like to buy you a drink somewhere neither of us has worked."* Disclosed his real first name, Risa's name and the circumstances of her death, the bracelet's evidentiary value, and his two reasons for not telling Scarlet in time to alter the trade. Accepted Scarlet's closure when she called Risa *girlfriend*. *"Thank you, Scarlet. I will move under my own name from wherever you set me down."*
- **The cockpit, *Phoenix Claw* (this hour).** Two small smiles. Named Bonadan as destination. Offered Pakuuni (Sirinno, the south road) as the piece of the universe he would give back.

## Knowledge
- **What he knows about the spiked spiral.** They bought the spring Coruscant trade-vote, owned the Aargau private vault, kept Risa's bracelet there because they could not afford to destroy it, contract through the Phantom Voice broker, maintain a list of names, run the Worlport salt house and Mortuda. He has not named the faction's in-faction name (presumably he does not know it; Vel called it *"us"*).
- **What he knows about Risa's killers.** A working theory, 18 months unfalsified. **He has not named the theory aloud to Scarlet.** The implication of *"The Aargau job was not entirely a job"* is that he believes the spiked spiral (or an adjacent party) was responsible. He has not confirmed.
- **What he knows about Scarlet.** Her name, her ship, her line (*alive, always*), her primary motivation (*"not because I love justice but because I want to see the universe"*), that she has worked for the spiked spiral twice and chose not to deliver him on the second contract, that she carries her professional restraint into intimate territory, that she pulled back when his grief became Scarlet's to hold. He has seen her drug a man, walk him through a Bothan concourse on a cover, renegotiate a 35k contract face-to-face, and uncuff a captive in front of armed strangers without losing the floor. He respects her. He has named that without saying it.
- **What he does NOT know.** Whether Scarlet has any longer-arc relationship with the spiked spiral that he should worry about; whether Cael Soros is going to surface; the contents of Brek'eevo's small-life pile in her kitchenette; her past before the work; the name and circumstances of the ex-lover who taught her to shoot.

## Working operational profile (the *Pyx* run, now ended)
- **Public record:** Skirran Emerald (Aargau, ~3 years ago, his signature heist), Tavros vault (Coruscant), Volann reliquary, Mannaki signet rings (Onderon), Nadrin Affair (first declared death), Bessi Run (the glass chamber — showmanship MO), Aargau private vault ~3 weeks ago (unpublished, spiked-spiral target).
- **Declared dead twice.** Never captured alive on record until Scarlet.
- **Skill profile:** vault and escape specialist. Reads locks, lifts objects, builds exits. Has at minimum Adept-tier in mechanical bypass / escape work; comparable in subterfuge. Reads rooms at Scarlet's level or better. Marksmanship unknown — his blaster has been a tool of last resort, not a working trade.
- **The run is over.** From Bonadan onward, Theron operates as Theron.

## What "Theron" implies (in-fiction, for the campaign)
The first name is enough to register a flat, take small jobs, build a small line of credit. The family name — which he has explicitly told Scarlet is hers *"the night you ask me a second time"* — is presumably tied to a longer paper trail: family of origin, possibly a Republic registration, possibly a court that has a warrant on a younger version of him. He has kept both halves off the boards equally, which means the family name carries weight of its own. **The full name is a campaign-level reveal pending Scarlet's choice.**
