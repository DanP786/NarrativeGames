# Risa (deceased)

## Status
Deceased ~18 months ago. Killed by an unknown party on the exit-route of a vault job she did with Theron. Body moved by the killers; Theron arrived at the meet point an hour after she was dead. **Theron has a working theory about who did it; 18 months unfalsified. Strongly implied (by Theron's confession that the Aargau hit was *"not entirely a job"*) to involve the spiked spiral or an adjacent party.**

## Description
Not given. Scarlet has not seen her, has no image, has no description beyond the silver mag-band bracelet she wore. **All Risa-canon flows through Theron's account.**

## Voice & mannerisms
Not given.

## Relationship to Theron
18 months partnership. *"Some of that was working; some of it was the part of working that is not."* — i.e. ~12 months as professional partner, the remainder personal as well. Wore his name (the part the boards do not have) and gave him hers. Was someone he could give his real name to.

## The bracelet
Plain silver, mag-band closure, with a working data-chip embedded in the underside (courier-style ID). The chip carried Risa's credentials and a year of her and Theron's job-tags. **Implicates the spiked spiral in a year of their shared work.**
- **Worn on the job that killed her.** On her wrist when she went out her exit route.
- **Not on her wrist when the killers moved the body.** Removed by them.
- **Recovered by Theron in the Aargau private-vault hit ~3 weeks ago.** The spiked spiral had been keeping the bracelet *"where they kept things they could not afford to destroy"* — the chip's evidentiary value against them outweighed their interest in destroying it.
- **Currently:** back in spiked-spiral hands. Scarlet traded it to Vel at the Worlport salt house, in the locked Aargau box, on 9 May (campaign-time). **Scarlet did not know what was in the box when she made the trade. Theron knew and did not tell her. He has named both reasons aloud.**

## The death — what Theron has named
- They had taken a box together. Their plan was to split on exit by two different routes.
- Theron took his; Risa took hers.
- The people whose box they had taken were waiting on Risa's route.
- They took her two hours after Theron was clear.
- An hour dead by the time Theron got back to the meet point.
- The body had been moved.

## What Theron has NOT named about the death
- The faction he believes did it (only implied — the Aargau-vault owners, i.e. the spiked spiral or an adjacent party).
- The world / station the job was on.
- What was in the box they had taken on that job.
- Why their plan called for two exit routes instead of one.
- Whether the wrong-call on routes was a tactical mistake or a betrayal.
- How long he searched for her killers before the Aargau plan came together.

## Long-arc thread
**Open.** Risa is a thread Theron has been carrying for 18 months and has now, in the trade-back of the bracelet, formally let go of as the operative motivator of his run. He has named that aloud. *"Trading it back was the first time in eighteen months I have been able to make a choice that put someone alive in front of someone dead."* This does not mean he will not pursue her killers — it means he has chosen, this one time, to let one choice be made the other way. Whether the bracelet, the chip, the year-of-tags, or Risa's killers ever come back into play depends on what Theron does in Bonadan and after, on whether Scarlet ever revisits the thread, and on whether the spiked spiral keeps or destroys the bracelet now that they have it back.

## Knowledge (durable canon points)
- Risa's job-tags from her year with Theron are on the chip in the bracelet. **If the spiked spiral keeps the bracelet rather than destroying it, the chip remains a piece of evidence that could come back into play.** (They had kept it for 18 months precisely because they could not afford to destroy it — that calculus has not changed.)
- The data-chip was, in Theron's words, *"her working ID, her identifiers, her job-tags from a year of our work."* The chip thus also implicates Theron in the same year of work. **Recovering the chip, were anyone to attempt it later, would recover evidence on both partners simultaneously.**
- Scarlet has handled the bracelet in oilcloth (when she packed the box in Tarvane's Quay) but never seen it. She has only Theron's description.
