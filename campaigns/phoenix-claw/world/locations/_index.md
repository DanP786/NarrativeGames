# Locations

*One line per known location. Deep files generated when a location matters.*

- Nar Shaddaa — the Smuggler's Moon, orbiting Nal Hutta. Departed S01 in haste; hostile network actively hunting Scarlet's face.
- Nar Shaddaa — *the Throat* (mid-level stack-tenement) — site of the Brek'eevo job; three of the killers' team left stunned in 7-J. Avoid.
- Nar Shaddaa — Dock 14-North, berth 7-left — *Phoenix Claw*'s recent berth. Departed clean S01.
- Ord Mantell — current destination, ETA ~14 hrs. Filed truthfully on the transit plan. Frontier port, Republic-aligned, working spacer trade, Scarlet has worked off the bounty board here before.
- Ord Mantell — **Kel Sahr port**, eastern continent. Small working port, low Republic presence, salvage and small-freight trade. Scarlet's landing point S01.
- Ord Mantell — **Worlport**, western continent. The planet's main city. ~6 hrs suborbital from Kel Sahr. Site of the **Iron Lung** cantina, docks district. Owner: an unnamed Mon Calamari who does not ask patrons their names.
- Ord Mantell — **Worlport salt-house district, deck nine of the old salt house.** Four-storey grey building, rusted plate, tired loading-bay light, fresh chalked spiked-spiral at the lintel each morning. **Spiked-spiral working meeting venue.** Two pedestrian doors (front, back-wall), concrete bay, square table centre, three chairs, work-lights at quarter output. Rotated outside lookouts (plain-coated, told not to remember faces). Site of S02 scene 5 — Vel's renegotiation meeting; Scarlet traded the Aargau box + spiral dossier for the Pyx bounty (35,000cr) and Theron's life and parole.
- Ord Mantell — **Mada's rooming house**, Worlport docks district. Four-storey flophouse, cash up front, no questions. Scarlet's current base — Room 4, third floor, two nights paid. Contract sheets stashed in the chest of drawers' false back.
- Ord Mantell — **The Iron Lung** cantina, Worlport docks district. Contract contact point for the Korel Avash bounty. Drink: *"a Tatooine sour, no salt."* Recognition: *"Vel sent me."* Selective weapons-check at the front; **side-door used by the Mon Calamari owner for discreet client handoffs.** Multiple hunters work contracts through the venue.
- Ord Mantell — **The Bent Hook** cantina, Worlport docks district. Working-shift joint with a window-bar facing the Iron Lung. Scarlet's scout position. Bothan waitress, no questions, 12cr for stew + caf + flatbread.
- Ord Mantell — **the yellow door, Kel Sahr working district** (two streets east of the small-craft pier). Unsigned tool-and-die shop fronting "the wrench's wife" (Dr. Mira Vesh) — discreet doctor.
- **Dolomar** — Mid Rim industrial world. Avash's registered home / business address. Postal-drop only.
- **Vella IV** — frontier mining world. Avash sighted ~3 months ago.
- **Trent's Reach** — non-aligned station. Avash sighted ~5 weeks ago (most recent).
- **Mortuda** — dead system, single uninhabited rock, no commercial activity for ~200 years. **Confirmed safehouse / dead-drop for the spiked-spiral faction.** Scarlet has delivered Avash here (S02). Rendezvous coordinate set by the dataspike; receiving party was an unnamed woman in plain working clothes. Scarlet has not seen the wider site beyond the meet.
- **Aargau** — Mid Rim banking world (known galactic location). Site of Pyx's signature Skirran emerald theft three years ago, and his most recent (3 weeks ago) private-vault job. Whatever Pyx took most recently came from Aargau.
- **Tarvane's Quay** — Bothan-run, smuggler-friendly deep-space station over the gas giant **Sothen** in the Bothan Reach. Four equatorial docking rings, town-sized, brown-and-gold plate-scarred hull. Out of Republic registries since before Scarlet was born. Information is the local currency; every Bothan you pass is pricing you. **Scarlet departed S02 scene 4 on a generic lift-off vector after capturing Pyx and stripping his flat; folded into transit-space 10 minutes after lift-off, no flight plan filed.** A Bothan data-trail is on the grid by morning (concourse-aide eye-witness, pad-7 deputy berth-log, maintenance-lift use) — readable by Cael Soros or the spiked-spiral if either thinks to buy. Will not be Pyx-friendly territory if Scarlet returns.
- Tarvane's Quay — **the Brassbird** — Bothan-keep dockside caf. Chitinous server-droid. Cheap local caf and flat-pastries. Working-class watering hole; info-traders solicit at the tables.
- Tarvane's Quay — **the Gold Hour** gambling hall. Pyx's haunt this week (seen twice in 4 days). He plays small and waits.
- Tarvane's Quay — **the Long Berth, deck four** — lodgings. Cael Soros (the other hunter on Pyx) is currently staying there.
- Tarvane's Quay — **the transit-deck observation strip** — wide curved walkway between deck levels, transparisteel wall waist-to-ceiling, long bench down the middle. Service-hatch access. Few people at off-hours. View: **Sothen** directly above, banded gold/copper/crimson, two small moon-shadows. Pyx's known quiet spot.
- Tarvane's Quay — **Bres's tailor, deck six** — Mon Calamari tailor's shop. Pyx had been sleeping in a flat above the shop under the alias *Mister Vahn.* **The flat is now empty** (S02 scene 4 — Scarlet packed it). Bres has seen Scarlet's face cleanly, twice.
- **Coruscant** — Republic capital, known galactic location. Site of the spring trade-vote scandal named in the spiked-spiral dossier — a bloc paid to break commitment, the vote killed, the reporting suppressed within a day. Avash was named once in the reporting. Pyx placed it from memory. **Politically significant to the spiked-spiral faction's interests.** Scarlet has not been (yet).
- **Pakuuni** — coastal world (referenced only). **Sirinno** — small port on a stretch of coast where the tide pulls back ~3km twice a day; locals walk out on the wet floor to pick blue urchins. The south road from Sirinno leads to where Theron spent a week between jobs not-working. Offered to Scarlet as *"the piece of the universe I would offer back."* Not yet visited.
- **Bonadan** — Corporate Sector trading world. Eastern spaceport, third quarter: a stretch of pawn-and-trade with light-but-incurious customs. Theron's destination at end of S02; he plans to be a man with his own name and a small line of credit inside a week. Theron has lived in a single cubic metre of a Bonadan dockside hostel for three months at a time during his run.
