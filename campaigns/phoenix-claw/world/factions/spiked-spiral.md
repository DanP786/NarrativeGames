# The spiked-spiral faction

*Vel's faction. In-faction name unknown. Vel called it *"us"* in S02 scene 4. The "spiked spiral" name is Scarlet's working label for them, taken from the wax-seal impression on their contracting paperwork and on the spring trade-vote dossier.*

## Identification
- **Wax seal:** small spiked spiral in red. Appears on Hutt-format contracting paper (the Brek'eevo / Avash contract that opened the campaign) and on the spring Coruscant trade-vote dossier Pyx had drafted from the Aargau vault.
- **Chalked sigil:** the same spiked spiral appears, freshly chalked, at the lintel of the Worlport salt-house deck nine on operating days.
- **Distinct from and at active operational conflict with the Vesper** (the plain-coat killer-team from S01).

## Confirmed reach (S02)
- **Mortuda** — dead system used as a safehouse rendezvous; pre-positioned receiver in plain working clothes; half-moon-token recognition protocol.
- **Ord Mantell** — the Iron Lung cantina (Vel's working venue for the Avash issue); the Worlport salt-house district deck nine (an operating room with rotated outside lookouts, two pedestrian doors, pre-staged satchels for both possible meeting outcomes).
- **Aargau** — private vault. Used for storing artefacts they cannot afford to destroy (Risa's bracelet with the chip). **Hit by Pyx ~3 weeks ago.** Whether they have moved the vault contents since the hit is unknown.
- **Bothan space** — relay-buoy infrastructure used both by the Phantom Voice broker for contracting and by the spring trade-vote bloc for paying the buy. Same infrastructure both times.
- **Coruscant** — the spring trade-vote scandal was their operation. A bloc paid (via the Bothan relay) to break a vote-commitment; the vote killed; the reporting suppressed within a day.
- **Korel Avash** — was their man. Avash was named in the suppressed reporting as part of the vote-bloc; the faction's *"A. killed July"* annotation in the dossier is the cover they built. Scarlet's protective extraction of Avash to Mortuda was their move to keep him alive after his cover-death was published.
- **The Phantom Voice** — anonymous-relay-buoy female broker; the faction's contracting arm. Same broker for the Avash and Pyx contracts.

## Doctrine & operational pattern
- **Hires through brokers (the Phantom Voice channel), not directly.** Vel is the in-person handler when face-to-face becomes necessary; she does not handle every contract.
- **Pre-stages multiple possible meeting outcomes.** Vel walked into the Worlport salt-house with a satchel ready under her chair before Scarlet had even named the renegotiation. The faction prepares for both *deliver-and-kill* and *trade-and-walk* and chooses based on what walks through the door.
- **Operates with rotated discipline at watch points.** Different lookout shifts mid-meeting at the salt-house; outside lookouts told not to remember faces.
- **Pays clean.** Both Scarlet contracts paid in full and on time. Vel's word held on the salt-house parole as far as the morning of the lift.
- **Maintains a "list of names."** Working hit/watch list. Vel referenced it openly: *"You stay off the list, you stay off the boards."* The list is presumably the running set of marks, witnesses, former assets, hostile competitors, and current targets. **Pyx (now Theron) has just been formally removed from it contingent on his staying off the boards.** Scarlet's name on the list is unknown — she has worked twice for them clean; she has also identified them; she has also seen Vel's face and used her own first name in front of two operatives. **Status: probably on the watch tier, not the target tier. For now.**

## Known personnel
- **Vel** — in-person handler at Ord Mantell. Mid-30s, sharp-faced, plain coat with darker cuffs, professional disclosure with controlled timing. Has now worked two clean jobs with Scarlet.
- **The Phantom Voice broker** — contracting arm. Female, even, no placeable accent. Audio-only. Anonymous relay buoy. Same broker for the Avash and Pyx contracts.
- **The Mortuda receiver** — plain working clothes, unnamed, female. Took Avash gently by the arm; no further contact.
- **The Worlport door-man, the back-wall man, and the rotating outside lookouts** (S02 scene 5) — all plain-coated, all professional, all silent.

## Politically significant interests
- **Spring Coruscant trade-vote** — bloc paid to break vote, vote killed, reporting suppressed in a day. *What was the vote?* Not yet known. The dossier did not name the bill. **Long-arc thread.**
- **Aargau private vault** — keeps items they cannot afford to destroy. Risa's bracelet was kept there. *What else is kept there?* Unknown.
- **The list of names.** Working watchlist. Theron is currently off it; Scarlet's status on it is unknown.

## Scarlet's working relationship
**Two clean jobs.** Avash (paid 20k, S02 mid-session). Pyx (paid 35k, renegotiated, S02 scene 5). Channel dark on the faction's side as of the lift from Kel Sahr. **Vel has not promised future contact; has not foreclosed it either.**
