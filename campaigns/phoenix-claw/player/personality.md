# How the galaxy sees Scarlet

## Reputation in general
Scarlet is a small name in a big trade. Working hunters in the Outer Rim ports she frequents recognise her — *the Kriov girl with the white ship and the alive-only rule* — but she has not done anything big enough to warrant a wider reputation. Few people on Coruscant or in the Hutt courts know her name.

The "alive only" thing has started to circulate. Reactions sort cleanly:
- **Some hunters:** principled. Respect it. May offer her work she would otherwise not be considered for.
- **Other hunters:** soft. Dismiss her. Will undercut her on jobs.
- **Some clients:** prefer her — they want the mark talking.
- **Other clients:** consider it a deal-breaker. Will not hire her.
- **Some marks:** know her name and run *toward* her, hoping she'll be the one who picks them up. Capture by Scarlet is survivable. Capture by the alternatives, often, isn't.

## By faction
- **Hutts:** A useful low-tier hunter. She has worked Hutt contracts on Nar Shaddaa and other Hutt-Space ports. None of the Hutts know her by name; their majordomos do.
- **Republic:** Off the radar. She has not held a Republic warrant. Her ship is registered legitimately; her papers are clean enough.
- **Bounty Hunters' Guild (such as it is):** Not a member. Works freelance.
- **Sith / Jedi:** Has not encountered either. Both would notice her work for different reasons if they did.

## Personal — the people who know her
- The ex-lover who taught her to shoot. Off-screen, alive somewhere, last system unknown to current narration.
- A handful of fences, dockmasters, and mechanics in three or four ports who know her name and will trade favours.
- No family. No partner. No crew.
