# Scarlet Kriov

## Vitals
- **Age:** 28
- **Species:** Human
- **Sex / gender:** Female
- **Origin:** Unspecified. Worked her way across the galaxy from a young age; no fixed homeworld she claims.
- **Profession:** Freelance bounty hunter, owner-operator. Mechanic by training.
- **Ship:** *Phoenix Claw* — light freighter / courier-class, ~25–30m. Cockpit, kitchenette, single bunk, refresher, holding cell, cargo space. Cramped, functional. Half-built by Scarlet herself.

## Appearance
Athletic build, slightly slimmed by long hauls and spacer rations. White, blonde shoulder-length straight hair. Tight white vest, black cargo trousers, boots, red crop bomber jacket. Two blasters strapped to the thighs — one stun, one lethal. Combat knife behind, on the belt.

## Backstory
Has grafted her whole life. Family killed in a skirmish she does not know the sides of — she was young, the details around it have stayed a fog and she has not asked them to clear. **Had an older sister.** Status / fate not yet established; possibly among the family she lost.

Before she got off her homeworld she ran a working-class kid's grift on newcomers to the spaceport — the unscrew-and-ransom hustle, lifting a small piece off a docked ship and selling it back as "found." She has the rhythm of a hustler in her past. She got herself off-world young.

She kept moving after that. Worked deck crews, freight yards, and eventually mechanic shops where she found she had a real hand for engines.

She saved for years. Half-built the *Phoenix Claw* with her own tools in her own time, paid for the rest in long contracts. The day she got it space-worthy she lifted off and did not look back.

The galaxy was the point. The bounty work is the means. She had picked up enough of how the trade worked from years around the kind of yards bounty hunters frequent. An ex-lover — a person from a few ports back — taught her to shoot when she said she was going to do this for a living. They have not been in the same system in some time.

## Current state
Watchful. Steady. Comfortable in her own ship and her own work; less comfortable when either of those is taken from her. Polite to the people she meets in the trade and quietly distant from most of them. She has friends in the way a working spacer has friends — names she trusts in three or four ports, people she would do a favour for and call due.

## The line
She brings them in alive. Always. She has never killed on a bounty. The lethal blaster is for if a job goes worse than she can handle non-lethally — she carries it, but she has not used it in earnest yet. Whether that holds is the campaign's quiet question.

## Goals
- See the galaxy. Different planets, different ports, different people.
- Keep flying. Fuel, port fees, repairs, food, in roughly that order.
- Hold the line — alive only.
- One day, maybe, ask what happened to her family. Not yet.
