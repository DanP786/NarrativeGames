# Skills

## Adept
- **Mechanic / shipwright** (10 ticks) — Her defining competence. She half-built the *Phoenix Claw* with her own hands, and there is no system on a small freighter she cannot keep alive with the right time and the wrong parts. Reads engines by sound. Fabs parts she cannot afford. Knows what most ships are lying about when they cough.
- **Streetwise** (15 ticks — Adept; realisation landed S03 scene 2, cockpit query; **+2 S04 climactic escape — read of corrugated cell, identified maintenance access under the handhold rail, working-spacer crawl-space + belly hatch, hull-shadow apron crossing**) — Has grafted in spaceport bottoms her whole life. Reads dockyards, fences, slicers, swoop-gangs, the bottom layer of any city. Knows which questions get answers and which ones get knives. Includes a drunk-girlfriend / wrong-door act for talking past doors that should not open for her, and the harder version of the same: walking a fully unconscious man through a populated Bothan concourse on the silhouette of "she is taking him home." Knows how to move through a hostile crowd at low profile — uses moving cover to break sightlines without telegraphing the evasion. Works the public faces of the HoloNet like a hunter — heraldic databases, postal-drop registrations, hunter-forum archive-trawling for scrubbed warnings. Can sit a target with a meal for an hour and come out with patterns. Has a face-and-posture toolkit she has deployed in many ports — and the discipline to use them without enjoying it. Has now landed the "single woman at the bar in the right dress" play against a never-captured target, walked him out of a station drugged, read a faction-handler's pre-staged satchel as a tell that both possible closings of a meeting were prepared, and renegotiated a contract worth the better part of forty thousand credits face-to-face in a concrete bay with two operatives at the walls.

## Novice
- **Piloting** (3 ticks) — Competent. She flies her own ship across the galaxy, runs atmosphere with a steady hand, knows the courier-lanes she works. Not an ace; will lose a dogfight to anyone who is. Better in the engine bay than the cockpit when push comes to shove.
- **Bounty work — non-lethal** (8 ticks — climactic capture +2 last session, +1 this session for the salt-house renegotiation; threshold to Adept = 10) — Knows the trade as a hunter. Specialised in stuns, snares, blunt takedowns, the occasional carbonite if she has access. Reads marks well enough. Picks her jobs at the easy end of the board on purpose. Has used the discipline against two armed professionals at once and put both down without lethal force; has drugged and walked out a never-captured high-skill escape artist (Pyx), recovery-position handled, carbonite-cuffed in her cell, all without firing a shot or breaking the mark's skin; and has now **renegotiated a 35,000-credit alive-delivery into a parole-and-walk-out for the target** in a faction meeting room. **The line — *alive, always* — has survived contact with people who wanted the man dead.**
- **Subterfuge** (6 ticks) — Reading a room, half-truths, knowing when to vanish. The dress-and-bar play, the third-face transition at the strip, the "he owes me dinner two months running" cover-line that walked her past a Bothan concourse-aide while dragging a drugged target, and the silent room-read at the salt house that put the trade together without exposing the bracelet. **The playing-possum on Berran's deck-plate** (eyes-third-open, body-still, listening) **and the silent apron-crossing at apron-spacer pace under three watching working ships** — both held without telegraphing. Working up.
- **Marksmanship — blasters** (9 ticks — **+3 S04 scene 4 climactic apron firefight: clean stun-drops on Mirai @ 60m and the third man @ 30m under returning lethal fire, plus the point-blank stun on Berran in his own cockpit**) — Trained by an ex-lover when she said she was going into the work. Steady with a stun pistol — has demonstrated a clean point-blank stun and a snap-shot across a small room under fire. Has also fired the lethal blaster on a job for the first time — a disabling foot-shot at point-blank while being choked from behind. The lethal is no longer just metal on her thigh.

## Notable Untrained
- **The Force** — not sensitive. The galaxy's deepest currents are closed to her; she cannot feel them and they do not feel her, beyond what an ordinary person registers as a wrongness when something Force-touched is in the room.
- **Killing** — not a deficit, exactly. A discipline. She has trained herself away from lethal force in the work. If the lethal blaster ever comes off her thigh in earnest, it will be clumsier than her stun-shooting.
- **Lightsabers / melee against Force-users** — she would lose. Knows it. Avoids it.
- **High society / Core etiquette** — has never set foot in a Senate gala and would not know how to hold the wrong fork.
