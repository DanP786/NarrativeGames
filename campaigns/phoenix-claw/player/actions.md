# Significant deeds

*The reputation log. Updated when Scarlet does something the galaxy will remember.*

- **Nar Shaddaa, the Throat, 7-J — the Brek'eevo job.** Walked in on the killing of her own bounty by a three-person professional team. Stunned two; disabled the third with a non-lethal foot-shot from the lethal blaster (**her first time using it on a job**); interrogated; left all three alive and stunned. Took the team's search target — a stack of flimsiplast that proved to be a private 25,000-credit alive-only contract on a man named **Korel Avash**, plus Brek's tracking ledger and a warning note. Did not collect from the Hutt majordomo Yelba. Her face is on a corridor cam in tenement 7-I — the recovering team will know what she looks like. The killers were Vesper.
- **Ord Mantell, Worlport, the Iron Lung — the Avash contract.** Activated the recognition phrase at the bar while the contract issuer (Vel) was sitting with the target. Saw Vel and Avash together; identified the contract as protective extraction under cover rather than ordinary bounty work. Told Vel the truth about how she got the contract — that the Vesper had killed Brek'eevo (and almost certainly Vel's first hunter Renno) and that Scarlet had taken the papers off them. Accepted the contract: 5,000 cr signing, 20,000 on confirmed delivery. Pickup window opens in 14 hrs.
- **Mortuda — the Avash delivery (resolved S02).** Picked up Avash at Worlport's old harbour 14 hours after the Iron Lung. Routed *Phoenix Claw* nine days through a path Vel had warned against. Did not encounter the Vesper. Delivered Avash to a woman in plain working clothes at the rendezvous coordinate on Mortuda; matched half-moon tokens; the handshake was unhurried. Avash nodded once before going. 20,000 cr cleared on confirmed delivery. **Did not see the Vesper at any point in the journey.** Covered her tracks cleanly on the return.
- **Three small jobs across two weeks (compressed).** Details unspecified. Standard alive-only work. Took the time to complete the three bacta sessions for the wrist herself.
- **Tarvane's Quay — the Pyx capture.** Beat **Cael Soros** to a never-captured-on-record vault-and-escape specialist by playing the single-woman-at-a-bar in a red dress at the Gold Hour. Dropped a slipsleep tab in his second drink, walked him out of the station under a drunk-girlfriend cover past a Bothan concourse-aide and the pad-7 dockmaster's deputy, lifted on a generic vector and folded ten minutes after. Also stripped his flat (clothes, kit, blaster, comm, datapad, bypass roll, credits, a sealed envelope of paper notes, and the small ornate metal box from Aargau wrapped in oilcloth in the duffel's false floor). **First time she has captured alive a target with a public reputation of escape.** A Bothan station data-trail (concourse-aide, dock deputy) is on the grid by morning — readable by Cael or the faction if either thinks to buy.
- ***Phoenix Claw* mid-transit — the dossier read; the spiked spiral identified.** Opened the sealed envelope from Pyx's flat. Read a four-sheet drafted confession of the spring Coruscant trade-vote scandal — a bloc paid through a Bothan-space relay buoy to break their vote-commitment, the reporting suppressed within a day. Wax seal: small spiked spiral, **same impression as the Hutt-format paper that carried the Brek'eevo contract** eight days before. Worked the fact-pattern. *The party that bought the vote, built Avash's death-cover, owns the Aargau vault Pyx hit, and is now paying for Pyx's recovery is the same faction.* **Identified her own employer.** Named the binding choice aloud at the bulkhead while Pyx watched her face change: she would not deliver.
- **Worlport, the salt-house deck nine — the renegotiation.** Walked Pyx into Vel's meeting cuffed, with the locked Aargau box and the spiral-sealed dossier in her pockets. Sat at the table opposite Vel and offered the trade: take both, pay me the bounty, let me walk Pyx out. Vel accepted. **Uncuffed Pyx in front of two faction operatives.** He opened the box without looking inside. Vel paid the 35,000 credits, took the satchel out the back door, and instructed the door-man to let them through. **Pyx formally off the faction's list of names contingent on staying off the boards.** Scarlet's working credit with Vel now stands at two clean jobs.
- **The closure — Phoenix Claw cockpit, Kel Sahr to Bonadan.** On the suborbital back, Theron disclosed his real first name (Theron), his late partner's name (Risa), the death-circumstances, the bracelet's evidentiary value, and that the Aargau hit was partly an act of revenge — the box had contained Risa's silver mag-band bracelet, which Scarlet had unknowingly traded back to the people Theron believes killed her. Scarlet pulled the intimate register back to the off-world drop, called Risa *girlfriend* rather than the name, and held the closure. **Held the *alive-always* line against the faction that had paid her and against herself when softening would have been easier.** Set vector for Bonadan, ~5-6 days transit. Theron will operate under his real name from Bonadan onward.
- **Vandros, Henn's Field apron — the Berran takedown.** Refuel stop went bad: pre-*Phoenix Claw* ex-lover Korro recognised her at the bar; Berran's freight-snatch crew took her with a sap-blow outside; she escaped from a corrugated holding cell through a maintenance hatch + sub-deck crawl; armed up at her own ship; **took down three of Berran's hands with stun-fire on the apron under returning lethal blaster fire from her own captured pistol.** Infiltrated Berran's lifting freighter via the belly hatch, came up on him in the cockpit, made him land on a hardpan north, stunned him point-blank, took his sidearm, walked the 40 minutes back to her ship in the dawn cold. ***Alive, always* held end-to-end. No kills. No collateral.** Berran (the boss) deliberately did not fire a shot the entire engagement; Scarlet stunned him in his own cockpit. Berran respected to the end as a working captain. Korro carried off Vandros by the bar-watchers afterward, whereabouts open. **Reputation: a freight-snatch crew that had been working the Aukris run for three years lost three to four hands, the boss's ship, and the score, against a single owner-captain who took no kills. That is the kind of story that travels apron-to-apron.**
- **Bonadan, eastern third quarter — the Devaronian freight-skip.** Four-day pursuit job for a third-quarter freight-line. Target had skipped a 9,000-cred debt and gone to the second-quarter pawn-stalls. Found him in a back-room sabacc game on the third day, walked him out at stun-pistol distance, delivered alive on the fourth morning. +4,000cr clean. Clean alive-only delivery on a working freight-line warrant.
- **Bonadan, eastern third quarter — the Sullustan off-world warrant.** Easier job. Target walked into a third-quarter clinic for a leg injury; the intake nurse filed the chit with the off-world warrant office. Scarlet picked him up at the clinic door and walked him to CSA processing the same day. +1,800cr. **Done by dinner.**
- **Bonadan to Tatooine, mid-fold — the Naia Tul contract accepted (delivery pending).** Took a Hutt-broker job from the Bonadan boards: Twi'lek female, late thirties, freight-line dispute with a junior Hutt called **Klornakka**. **Alive only with the *answer-the-question* clause — the Hutt will torture her after delivery.** 1,800cr. *The captain at twenty-five would not have taken this one.* Scarlet took it knowing the clause. *"I am going to deliver her. And the Hutt is going to do what the Hutt is going to do. I have done it before. It pays the bay-rent."* **First contract she has accepted that crosses into the *delivery to torture* register since the player began tracking her conscious cost-of-the-work.** Main-thread relevant. Delivery pending at session-end.
