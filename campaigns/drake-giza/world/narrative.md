# Narrative

Nathan Drake, retired four years to a beach house on the Florida coast, takes a call from Jessica Locke — investigative journalist, old debt from Yemen 2014 — on a phone he hasn't used in eighteen months. She is in Cairo. She is being followed. She has a 1973 CIA file that no one was supposed to see, and the people she found it for have noticed.

The story of what Drake does with that call.
