# Significant deeds

*The reputation log. Pre-campaign deeds in summary; current-campaign deeds appended as they earn it.*

## Pre-campaign
- **~2007, Panama and the Pacific.** Found El Dorado. It was not a city of gold; it was something worse. Drake destroyed the sarcophagus. Sully knows. Elena knows. No one else who matters is alive to.
- **~2009, Borneo, Nepal, Tibet.** Found Shambhala. Lost it, the sap, and Zoran Lazarević. Got Elena out of the Himalayas alive. The Tibetan rubbings still hang in the kitchen at the beach house.
- **~2011, Yemen and the Rub' al Khali.** Found Iram of the Pillars. Watched Marlowe drown in the desert and watched the city sink under the sand. The marriage cracked here; healed afterward, slowly.
- **~2017, Madagascar and Libertalia.** Found Avery's island. Reunited with Sam, who he had thought dead for fifteen years. Killed Rafe Adler in single combat on a sinking ship. Retired immediately afterward and meant it.
- **2014, Yemen (separate).** Jailed on a frame-up over a Sabaean tablet. Jessica Locke pulled him out via Sully in 48 hours instead of 18 months. Owes her one. The debt is current.
