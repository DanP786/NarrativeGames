# Skills

## Adept
- **Climbing & free-running** (14 ticks) — walls, ledges, rooftops, scaffolds, the iconic Drake-shaped wall-of-ruins ascent. Trained on the streets of Cartagena before he was old enough to know what climbing meant, refined across two decades of work. The shoulder is not what it was.
- **Gunfighting** (16 ticks) — pistols and carbines under pressure. Not a sniper, not a marksman — a survivor of fifty firefights who shoots fast and accurate at close range, conserves rounds, reloads on the move. Has not fired a weapon at a human being in 18 months. Will remember how.
- **Historical research & antiquities** (12 ticks) — Sully-schooled and field-tested. Reads enough Latin to argue with a scholar, enough hieroglyphs to recognise a cartouche, enough provenance paperwork to spot a forgery in under a minute. Cannot translate cold but can find a translator who can. Has a working library at the beach house that Elena pretends not to notice growing.

## Novice
- **Languages** (3 ticks) — broken Spanish (good for street negotiation, bad for poetry), broken French, enough Arabic to get punched in three countries, fragments of Mandarin, a useful amount of Italian.
- **Brawling** (4 ticks) — fists, elbows, anything to hand. Has been in too many bar fights for a man with a daughter.

## Notable Untrained
- **Stealth at distance** — he's a chaos magnet. Works close, not quiet. Sniping is not a skill he has any business pretending to have.
- **Anything resembling a normal life** — sitting still, holding a desk, eating dinner without looking out the window. The work always finds him, partly because he leaves the line open.
- **Diving past recreational depth** — recreational, fine. Technical, no. Don't put him in a wet suit at 60 metres without backup.
