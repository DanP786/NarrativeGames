# Campaign setup

Created: 19 May 2026 (real-world date)
Slug: drake-giza

## Onboarding answers

**Genre / references:** Uncharted (the games). Nathan Drake style — globe-trotting treasure hunting, pulp adventure, ancient legends that turn out to be uncomfortably real, banter as a survival skill.

**Voice:** 3rd person, present tense. *"Drake ducks behind the pillar as the rope snaps tight."* Held consistent regardless of how the player phrases prompts.

**Length:** Structured, short (~12 scenes). Three-act planned arc.

**Tone:** Gritty pulp. Uncharted's shape but the consequences bite. The supernatural register stays under spook-thriller plausibility until late (~scene 9-10), then breaks.

**Content posture:** Nothing off the table. Shown with weight, not relish.

**Stakes / scope:** History-shaking find. The relic is real, the find rewrites maps or governments, powerful people will bury bodies to control the outcome.

**Agency posture:** Realist-gritty. Drake is competent, not invincible. The plan goes sideways more often than it goes right.

**Death rule:** Hard death enabled. Drake can die.

**Mind-reading rule:** None. Thoughts are private.

**Main thread (sketch):** See `meta/main-thread.md`. An old journalist friend, Jessica Locke (a fan turned colleague who got Drake out of a Yemeni jail in 2014), calls him from Cairo about a 1973 CIA archaeological program at Giza — a deniable joint operation now reactivated as a CIA/CCP black-budget effort, chasing whatever Khufu's Great Pyramid was actually *for*. Six names on the program inventory; five dead; the sixth redacted.

**Character seed:**
- Nathan "Nate" Drake. 43. Retired four years post-Libertalia to a beach house on the Florida coast with Elena and their daughter Cassie (10).
- Salvage business with Sully out of Key West is cover, but mostly a polite fiction now. He has not lifted anything from anywhere in 18 months.
- The phone in the kitchen drawer is his one concession — he hasn't used it, but he hasn't gotten rid of it either.

**Skill kit (confirmed):**
- Adept: Climbing & free-running, Gunfighting, Historical research & antiquities
- Novice: Languages, Brawling
- Notable Untrained: Stealth at distance, anything resembling a normal life, diving past recreational depth

**Opening hook:** The call. Late evening, the beach house. Elena upstairs, Cassie asleep. The burner rings for the first time in 18 months. Jessica Locke, London-quiet, from a hotel in Zamalek: *"I need you to come to Cairo."*
