# How the world sees Mira

*Updated through S05 (~Day 9). She is no longer a blank to the camp — she is the bonded sky-woman who defied chief and matriarch and came home kept and silent.*

## By tribe
- **Vahn-rii (broadly):** The first of the sky-women, and now visibly a *mate* — Korren's, the bond made in the rock. The camp counted her among the made-women whose arrival frightens them (the sky still dropping more). Read on the return as a bonded woman who *came home to her man* and would not answer the tribe's questions — proper, by their law, and unsettling, by Senna's reading. Object of fear-of-the-unknown more than hostility.

## By the Kael'tha (individuals)
- **Korren** — *Devoted / bonded.* His mate; secured, permanent possession. Reads her clinging and deference as proof the bond is true; will end anyone's attempt to scrutinise her. Chose, in the lodge, to trust her *I don't know* and "not-know together."
- **Senna** — *Protective, now shaded with pity.* Sees a woman *made easy to keep* and *taught not to speak*; blames the hand that made her, not Mira. The truest potential ally and the one closest to the dangerous truth.
- **Drenn** — *Wary, and now reliant.* His Day-2 prophecy about her came true; he suspects she understands the thing that took him, and wants it from her.
- **Thaal** — *Neutral; weighs her.* The woman who spoke his tongue the first night and said "thousands"; a question about the tribe's safety, to be judged, not befriended.
- **Eshen** — *Fond.* The child who was won by the water-work and ran to Mira on the party's return (then balked at Korren). The warmest, simplest tie Mira has.

## By the other women (her own kind)
- **Ada Okonkwo** — *Fractured / distrustful.* Was the kin-hand; now reads Mira as compromised by the bond ("it makes you its advocate"), and clocked that Mira hid the neck-implant from them. Level, final: *you gave in; I'm not going to.* The counter-pole to Mira's surrender.
- **Mari Quintero** — *Wounded, but still leans on her.* Felt betrayed by Mira's *maybe Drenn can be that for you* — yet Mira is still the only one who told her the truth and speaks to her gently.
- **Suvi Lindqvist** — *Kin, reaching, unanswered.* Exchanged *I see you*; reached for Mira by her true name and was put off. The hand Mira hasn't taken. Tracked and counted the returning party.
- **Ren Castellanos / Liesel Varga** — No real contact yet; held separately at camp.

## Reputation themes (what precedes her now)
- **The bonded one / the kept one.** Visibly, publicly Korren's.
- **The defiant one.** Half of the pair that broke the chief's and matriarch's word and ran for the forbidden dark.
- **The silent one.** Would not answer the reckoning; read variously as guilty, broken, or *taught not to speak.*
- **The one who knows (suspected).** Drenn and Senna both sense she understands more than she says; Ada *knows* she withheld.
- **Competent hands.** Among those who've seen it (Korren; Eshen; the tended women), the water and the triage mark her as more than the frightened thing she looks — which, in Korren's mouth, only makes her *worse to lose.*
