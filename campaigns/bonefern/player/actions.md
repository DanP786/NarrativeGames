# Significant deeds

*The reputation log. Updated when Mira does something the world will remember.*

- **Day 1, on the canopy.** Gave her name to Korren — first word she has spoken on Vethaal. *"Mira."*
- **Day 1, at Thaal's command.** Lifted her face to the chief but did not meet his eyes. The camp registered it.
- **Day 1, at Senna's fire.** Did not speak the entire time she was being washed, dressed, fed. Senna noted it openly: *"You have not spoken since you came in."*
- **Day 1, at the central fire.** Sat at Korren's claim without contesting it. When the camp stood at the arrival of the three other women, she stood too — and let Korren pull her against him in front of the chief, the senior scout, and the temple-marked young woman who had been watching her all evening. Read by the camp as acceptance.
- **Day 1, in Korren's lodge.** First volunteered words on Vethaal were a question about the *other women,* not about herself. Korren marked it.
- **Day 1, in Korren's lodge.** Allowed Korren to undress her, set her, take her body in the way Vahn-rii custom permits before the cave. Initiated touch herself — first time on her own decision since waking. **First sex on Vethaal,** with the cave-penetration taboo intact.
- **Day 2, at Korren's fire (his absence).** Drenn crossed to test her and handed her the *deep places* to wound; she kept Korren's rule and gave the senior scout nothing. He ceded the fire.
- **Day 2, before Thaal.** Took the controlled account through Korren's channel; learned, in the same breath as the chief, that a made thing is sealed in her neck. Kept her face still.
- **Day 2, in Senna's lodge.** Confided the translation-chip theory and accepted the matriarch's offered alliance, woman to woman — her first chosen tie on Vethaal that is not a claim.
- **Day 2, in Korren's lodge.** Covered for Senna, then partly broke: gave Korren the *placed-to-be-taken* truth while holding back the warning about him. A first real act of managed disclosure to the male who keeps her.
- **Day 2, in Korren's lodge — the choice.** Offered one narrow chance to refuse the path, **chose it** — *"I would like to see these caves"* — under a shadow neither she nor Korren can lift: whether the choosing is hers or was grown in her.
- **Day 2, late morning — the cleaned mark.** With Korren turned away, washed his claiming-mark from her own skin. A small private act of self-keeping. Korren saw it and banked it.
- **Day 2, late morning — the water.** Alone and obedient to Korren's *stay,* diagnosed the camp's foul standing water and built an improvised char-and-sand filter (Eshen fetched, Vesh gave char), producing clean water — the **first demonstration of her life-support competence on Vethaal.** Won Eshen; Vesh half-aware. A path to standing that is *hers.*
- **Day 2, evening — chose Korren over the kin-hand.** Suvi reached across the camp through Eshen, openly, using Mira's *true name,* asking how the water was made clean. Mira put her off to *"the morning"* and left that night instead.
- **Day 2, night — the run taken.** By her own decision (*"We go"*), left the Vahn-rii camp in secret with Korren for the forbidden deep places, **against the word of Thaal and the verdict of Senna.** Open defiance, once the furs are found cold.
- **Day 4, the high river — her hands saved them.** Read the flood like a failing system and **called the safe crossing herself,** caution over speed — *"being caught is better than dead."* Korren honoured it though it cost an hour and gave it to Drenn. *"Your hands were right."* The first time her competence *saved* them — witnessed only by Korren.
- **Day 5, the deep places — claimed, and the bond made.** Crossed the threshold of the forbidden caves **of her own will** and let Korren claim her fully; the bond is made to him and to Vahn-rii custom. **THE HIDDEN DEED (only Mira half-knows):** at the height of it the implant *activated* — the spore reached through it and *took.*
- **Day 5, the cave mouth — stopped a chamber with a word.** With Korren coiled to kill and Drenn intruding, Mira de-escalated the whole standoff with one chosen word — *"Wait. Let's go outside and talk"* — *without* stepping in front of Korren (speaking, not challenging). Won leave to tend the new women on his three conditions and gave him the whole *yes.* A public demonstration that the bonded woman has a steadying voice — and is kept.
- **Day 5, the stream — tended her kin, and lost one.** Used her competence to keep Ada & Mari alive (water, washing, food), and gave them the truth in the human tongue (*it's biology; it isn't you*) — withholding the implant. Then, asked for a way out, she **advocated the bond** (*"I gave in to it. I like Korren. He makes me feel safe"*) and urged Mari toward Drenn. **Ada named it** — *that's the thing talking; it makes you its advocate* — and the kin-alliance fractured. The women now know where Mira stands: she leans in. Ada distrusts her.
- **~Day 9, the chief's terrace — silent before the reckoning.** Brought home in open defiance, exposed by Senna as the one who *knew,* Mira stayed silent and eyes-down through the whole reckoning, deferring entirely to Korren, who claimed her out of the interrogation (*"she is kept"*). The camp saw a bonded woman come home to her mate and refuse the tribe's questions; Senna read it as a woman *taught not to speak* and pitied her.
- **~Day 9, Korren's lodge — the unmanaged truth.** Alone, asked what is in her neck and what she knows, Mira answered honestly for the first time since the moss — *"I don't know what it is… or who put it there, or what it's for."* True; she kept back only her guesses. Korren found no seam and chose to *not-know with her.* The bond's trust deepened on a foundation of (near-)honesty — and Mira's last managed secret narrowed to a single shut door.
