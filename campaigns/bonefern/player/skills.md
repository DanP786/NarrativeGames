# Skills

## Adept
- **Filtration & life-support systems** (14 ticks) — Her defining competence. Water, air, contamination, improvised filters; and, increasingly, field life-support *triage* on people. On Vethaal: clean water, poisons, what air not to breathe, improvising; rehydrating and cleaning up the half-dead. *(Latest tick — S05, the stream: chose the live self-cleaning flow on instinct, paced two dehydrated women's drinking against shock, cleaned an infected wound with water and a thumb without flinching. The body is just another system.)*

## Novice
- **Reflex-first movement** (2 ticks) — Acts before thinking. Can save her or send her the wrong way.
- **Practical hands** (3 ticks) — Knots, splicing, basic improvisation; filtration-tech fingers that find the working end of a knot by feel.
- **Watching from the edge** (3 ticks) — Shy-person observation; reads rooms, tells, and the gaps between what people say and do. *(Latest tick — S05: read the whole terrace reckoning — Senna's pity, Korren's cracked certainty, Ada's level verdict — without saying a word.)*
- **Speaking up** (2 ticks — *newly crossed from Untrained, S05; fragile*) — She still knows what she wants to say and the getting-it-out is still the hard part — but under real pressure this session she did it, repeatedly and to effect: the single chosen word that stopped a chamber coiled to violence (*"Wait."*), naming a bonding male to halt him (*"Drenn?"*), laying out the hard truth to the other women, and — hardest — the first wholly unmanaged honesty she has given anyone since the moss (*"I don't know what it is…"* to Korren). **Caveat:** it is situational and it fails under shame — mid-S05 she also retreated all the way back into mute deference when a room turned on her. A real but unreliable new capacity, not a settled trait.

## Notable Untrained
- **Combat of any kind.** Has never thrown a punch in earnest. Freezes if directly menaced unless reflex-first kicks in.
- **Climbing.** Not athletic that way.
- **Wilderness survival as a learned discipline.** Filtration knowledge carries some load; tracking, fire-making by hand, foraging without ship-database confirmation, predator avoidance are not yet in her hands. *(She will NOT dose drinking water with botanicals she can't identify — competence and flora-ignorance both intact.)*
- **Anything athletic.** Strong-thighed, not weak, not fit. A long sprint will gas her; she cannot hold a Kael'tha pace.
