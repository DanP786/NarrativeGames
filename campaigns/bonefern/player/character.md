# Mira Holloway

## Vitals
- **Age:** 26
- **Origin:** Earth-born, colony-track. Spent most of her adult life shipboard.
- **Last assignment:** Filtration tech, colony ship *Helia-Voss*. Water systems primary, air systems secondary. Three years on that ship; six years shipboard total.
- **Build:** 5'2". Hourglass — large breasts, narrow waist, wide hips, thick thighs, fine ankles and wrists. The kind of body that did not fit standard-issue jumpsuits well.
- **Skin:** Warm brown. Holds colour easily.
- **Hair:** Black, wavy — curls tighter when wet, frizzes in humid air. Worn long, past the shoulder blades.
- **Eyes:** Dark brown, large and expressive — they give her away.
- **Marks:** Small sealed implant at the base of her skull, hidden under the hair — known since Day 2 (Senna found and named it). As of the cave it is **awake and has *activated*** at least once: the moss/spore reached *through* it during the claiming. Mira knows the device is real and tied to the bond; she does not know it is the route, or fully what was done through it. (Canon: it is also the translation implant; all six women carry one.)

## Backstory
Grew up in one of the orbital crèches that fed the colony program — institutional, not unkind, not warm. Tested into a technical track at fourteen and into filtration at seventeen. Liked the work because it was unsupervised and quiet: a malfunctioning regulator did not care that she had not said good morning. The *Helia-Voss* was her third ship. She kept her quarters tidy, kept her head down, ate alone more often than not, and was three months from rotating off when she remembers being in a transfer corridor and seeing a face she could not place. She does not remember anything between then and waking up here.

## Personality
Shy, quiet, watchful. Reads rooms before she walks into them. Speaks less than she thinks. Hates being looked at. Underneath that — and she does not always like this about herself — she is reflex-fast: when something startles her she moves before her brain has finished. *(S05 note: under real pressure she has begun, unreliably, to push past the shyness and speak to effect — and also to collapse all the way back into mute deference when a room turns on her. Both are now true of her.)*

She has had two relationships, both short, both with men. Neither prepared her for any of this.

## Current state (~Day 9, the Vahn-rii camp)
- **Bonded mate to Korren.** Walked the path of her own will (S03–S04), was claimed fully in the deep places, and the implant fired during it. Dressed again in the dust-red wrap; at Korren's lodge at the camp.
- **Has found that she likes being kept** — the possession reads to her as safety after a life unseen — and in S05 she said so aloud and even counselled it to another captive. She cannot tell where her own wanting ends and the thing in her neck begins, and is increasingly too tired to keep grieving the question.
- **Carries the campaign's heaviest secret only half-shared:** she knows the bond is biological / cave-borne ("it isn't you"), and has told the other women that much — but she has *not* told them they're chipped, and told Korren honestly she doesn't know what the implant is, keeping back only her guesses.
- **Fracture with Ada** (who reads her as compromised) and a wounded thread with **Mari** (whom she counselled toward Drenn). **Senna** pities her as a woman taught not to speak. The deferred **reckoning** with the chief waits for morning.

## Goals
- Survive — and survive what was done to her in the cave, which she can't yet name.
- Understand what she actually is, stripped of the ship and the uniform — and now, what the made-thing in her neck is *for.*
- A foothold of competence and standing that is *hers* (the water; tending the women).
- Underneath, increasingly owned: to be **kept** by Korren — while not being able to trust that the wanting is her own.
- (Unfaced) reckon with whether her surrender is costing the other women their chance to resist.
