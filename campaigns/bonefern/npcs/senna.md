# Senna

## Status
Alive. Older Vahn-rii woman, matriarch-tier — custodian of new women, keeper of arrival-rites, reader of the will of the deep places. Both wisewoman and headwoman. Day 2 (S03): declared Mira's sealed implant **awake** and forbade the caves while it is so; carried the verdict to Thaal. **S05 (~Day 9): the run she feared happened — Korren defied the verdict, the bond was made, and the party returned with two more women. At the terrace Senna found the made-thing on a SECOND woman (Mari) and began reasoning aloud toward the truth.**

## Description
Tall as Vahn-rii women run, no longer young. Long rope of grey-bronze hair over one shoulder. Arms marked head-to-wrist in fine dyed/scarred traceries (older patterns than natural luminescence). Wrap crossing both shoulders. No weapon Mira has seen. Dry grip, warm and stronger than her size promises.

## Voice & mannerisms
Quiet, lower than expected for her frame; never raises it; lands harder than the chief's at half the volume. Speaks as if she has had every conversation in front of her at least once. Pointedly does **not** look at Korren when she moves against his wants — the absence of the look is the statement. (Broke that rule once at the cord, deliberately, to refuse the cut — *"spent the look like a struck blow."*)

## Relationship to Mira
**Friendly / protective**, deepened in the cord scene into something near guardianship — and **shaded in S05 with pity.** She put her body and standing between Mira and two of Korren's wants in one morning (the cut; the caves), and gave the truest counsel Mira has had. In S05, watching Mira return bonded, exposed, and silent, Senna read the silence not as guilt but as a woman *taught not to speak* — and pitied her, openly, which is harder for Mira to bear than the old accusation. Senna does not blame Mira for the run; she blames the hand that made her.

Her core counsel to Mira (still standing):
- On the implant: *"A thing meant to help you would not need to be hidden from you."*
- On Mira's nature: **made easy to keep** — serving equally a woman *loved into a tribe* or a quiet unwitting *eye.*
- On Korren: *"Good is not the same as safe… Keep something of yourself, even from him."*

## Goals & pressures
- Read and protect the new women; the man's claim is the man's, the woman's readiness is the women's.
- **Understand the made-things before more women are bonded.** Her watching-plan was broken by the secret run; S05 hardened her purpose. She has now felt the implant on two women and intends to **examine the new ones** (Ada & Mari).
- Hold the camp together through a frightening arithmetic (two more women; the sky still dropping them) without letting fear turn to a purge.
- Keep the women's reasoning from the men where it serves the women.

## History with Mira
- Day 1: received, fed, wrapped her; felt something at the nape, said nothing.
- Day 2 (S03): named the sealed thing; mapped it; reasoned past the *translation-chip* theory; offered alliance; the **cord scene** — found the implant **awake**, refused Korren's proposed cut (lethal, against the spine), set the price of permission, carried the verdict to Thaal.
- **S05 (~Day 9, the terrace):** met the returning party — Korren bonded, Drenn lit and claiming a stranger, two new women, Mira marked and silent. Crossed to **Mari**, made Drenn let go, and found the **nape made-thing, twin of Mira's.** Reframed it before the chief: every sky-woman carries the same made-thing by the same hand; *or the made-thing is how it chose* — put there on purpose, *"the deep places' — or something wearing the deep places like a skin."* Exposed Mira (*she has known since I found hers*), then read her silence as a *silenced* woman and pitied her aloud (*"She has been taught not to. That tells you something too."*).

## Knowledge
- Knows Vahn-rii custom around new women in full.
- Knows the implant is real, **awake, and reactive to a claiming male's nearness**; has now confirmed it on **two** women (Mira, Mari). Holds the *easy-to-keep* and *eye-or-beloved* readings, and has now reasoned to a further, sharper shape: the women were **made on purpose for this**, by *"a hand wearing the deep places like a skin"* — an outside will using the sacred dark. **She is the in-world mind closest to the spore-truth, in her own frame; she has no sci-fi vocabulary for it and does not know the word "spore."**
- Knows (S05) that Korren defied the verdict, reached the rock, and is bonded; that Drenn was taken by the dark too; that Thaal has claimed Ada.
- Flags **Suvi** as the one most likely *aimed.*
- Does **not** know the true total number of women; does **not** consciously know the spore, the implants-as-translation-route, or that anything was "taken" from Mira in the cave. Does not know Mira's withheld guesses.
