# Korren

## Status
Alive. Adult male, Vahn-rii (river-walker tribe). Claimed Mira before the chief on arrival (Day 1). S04 (Days 3–5): walked her four days to the deep places, held his pre-cave rule to the threshold, then claimed her fully in the rock — the bond made, in his eyes. S05 (Day 5 → ~Day 9): shielded Mira, defused contamination suspicion via the sacred frame, granted her leave to tend the new women on three conditions, led the party four days home, claimed her out of the terrace reckoning, asked his question in the lodge and chose to *not-know together.* **S06 (Day 10 morning): woke after, accepted her morning gift, dressed her himself in Vahn-rii mated-wife attire, walked her down to the chief's lodge openly marked. In council before the half-circle of older males he refused Thaal's *saying* over Ada and INVOKED THE ROCK against the chief — first public sign of his privately cracked certainty. Yelka broke the council (*"he was to be mine"*) and snatched at Mira's cord; Korren pried her off finger by finger without breaking her, and the single knot he had tied at Mira's right hip held. Accepted Thaal's trade: rock for Ada, in return for a public acknowledgement to Yelka via Vora before the smoke turns black. Refused Thaal's "how did you make her so" with the principled answer: *the rock made her mine. She has made the rest.* — publicly framing Mira's behaviour as Mira's own. Marked Mira's withdrawal during the council; owes her later. Now en route to Vora.**

## Description
Tall, lean and athletic — a river-walker's swimmer-and-climber build, not a brawler's. **Grey-green** pebbled skin, darker down the spine and forearms. Long pointed ears; vertical-slit pupils, eyes **dark amber**. Wet near-black hair swept back. Retractable claws (kept retracted around Mira; also used patiently to cut and stitch hide); fangs at the jaw. **Green-teal** bioluminescent tracery runs the outsides of the arms, collarbones, sternum — faint in daylight, brighter with feeling (he can't govern it). Brown hide hip-wrap, knotted cord belt; blade at the small of his back. *(Appearance locked to reference art.)*

## Voice & mannerisms
Quiet; does not raise his voice. Short complete sentences. Unblinking eye contact. Says her name carefully. Handles her deliberately, without negotiation or apology. Body-hum constant — **his hum matches Mira's** (the unrecognised spore-bond signal; since the cave the two are one note); carries an **edge** when angry, drops to a low *here-with-you* pitch with her, goes **flat/silent — held, not gone — when most dangerous** (killed his light to nothing when Drenn intruded in S05). **In council he does not raise it because he does not have to.**

## Relationship to Mira
**Devoted / bonded (mate)**, in S05 deepened on a new axis: **trust.** **In S06 deepened on two more:** *public claim* (the dressing, the walk down, the mate-marking before the camp) and *principle* (in front of his chief and the camp's older males he framed Mira's behaviour as *hers,* not his work: *the rock made her mine; she has made the rest*).

He reads her clinging, her deference, her coming-home-to-him as proof the bond is true — and it pleases him even when (as Ada saw) it is also the bond pacifying her. **S06 added small new things he files as *hers* and not as a woman of his people would do:**
- Mira **waking before him** and pleasuring him awake (*"no woman of mine has ever done that"*).
- Mira **reaching for his hand** unprompted on the way out of the lodge (*as if he had been expecting her always to do this*).
- Mira **going to him** at his *"come here"* without his moving toward her.
- Mira **watching his face the whole way through the dressing.**

Each of these pleased him at a register that is not the ordinary register of being pleased.

**Possessive to the bone — and diagnosed.** The cord scene proved it: the threat to Mira's *life* didn't move him; the threat to their *connection* did. **S06 tested the cord physically** — Yelka pulled it; the single knot Korren tied at Mira's right hip held. He freed it without breaking the woman who pulled. Gentle, and gentle is **not** safe.

**He has marked Mira's withdrawal at the council** (her hand leaving his wrap, her arm wrapping her own ribs). Did not address it; the morning would not let him. **Owes her later.**

## Goals & pressures
- Keep and protect his bonded mate; control what the camp learns of her.
- **Make the Yelka acknowledgement** before the smoke turns black — pay the price for the rock he just put on Thaal.
- Watch Ada-to-the-rock play out without Mira getting drawn in.
- **Live with a crack he won't name** — cracked by Senna in S05; tested again by Thaal's *how* question in S06 (which he refused without examining too closely whether the refusal was an answer or a flinch).

## History with Mira (Day 1 → S06)
- **Day 1–2:** found her, claimed her, gave Thaal a controlled account, faced down Drenn, established the private channel, refused to let her wash his mark, owned a fault, committed to the secret run.
- **Day 2 cord scene (S03):** heard the implant declared awake, proposed the cut, was stopped, heard Senna's *good-is-not-safe.*
- **S04 (Days 3–5):** the four-day run; the river (*"your hands were right"*); the threshold; the claiming and (unknown to him) the activation.
- **S05 (Day 5 → Day 9):** woke and kissed her nape (felt only his own light surge); shielded her when Drenn intruded; weaponised the sacred frame; granted her the tending-bargain; led the return; claimed her out of the interrogation (*"she is kept"*); in the lodge asked his question, accepted her *I don't know,* chose to not-know together, held her without claiming.
- **S06 (Day 10 morning):**
  - Slow, mutual, chosen lovemaking deep into the night; held her sleeping with the bond-hum one note.
  - Woke to find Mira had risen first and was pleasuring him — *"no woman of mine has ever done that"* — and was deeply moved by her *"you don't need to do anything to be thanked"* (*"I think I would like to believe it of you"*).
  - Went to the women's lodge and fetched the **Vahn-rii mated-wife outfit** (river-green hide skirt, shoulder-tied top, **river-stone bead cord, the mate-mark**). Returned and dressed her himself, hands-first, ending with the cord twice at her waist with **the practised single knot of his people at her right hip.** *"Now you are dressed as what you are."*
  - Took her hand without looking down at the gesture (*as if he had been expecting her always to do this and not as a woman of his people would*) and walked her down through the waking camp to the chief's lodge, openly mate-marked. Briefed her: *"Eyes on my face when he speaks. Not on his. Mine."*
  - In council: refused Thaal's *saying* without bond; **invoked the rock** against the chief's bondless claim on Ada (*"the binding is the rock"*).
  - When Yelka broke the council and snatched at Mira's cord: **rose at once,** pried Yelka's fingers off without breaking her, the knot at the right hip held; spoke once: *"You will let go of her."* Let Yelka take her own arm back; gave her the dignity of leaving on her own legs.
  - Accepted Thaal's trade (rock for Ada; Yelka acknowledgement before the smoke turns black, via Vora). *"What I do in front of her, you will see."*
  - Refused Thaal's *"how did you make her so"* with: *"There is no what. There is the rock. There is the keeping. There is the speaking of her name. … She has made the rest. There is no other answer I have for you. And I would have one if there were."* — publicly framed Mira's behaviour as her own.
  - **Marked Mira's withdrawal** when she released his wrap and wrapped her own ribs; did not address it; owes her.

## Knowledge
- Knows Mira's stated origin (a ship beyond the sky; her people; the women likely one vessel) and believes it.
- Knows a made thing is sealed at her nape (Senna named it; *"it makes you easier to keep"*); knew before S05 that it is **awake and reactive to his nearness.** S05 added Senna's louder claim — *a hand wearing the deep places like a skin* — and a second made-thing on Mari. S06 has yet to add anything beyond reaffirmation; the count of *two confirmed* sits without him chewing it.
- **Believes the bond is *made* and *true*** (the rock woke; the two hums are one). Does **NOT** know it is the moss/spore. Does **NOT** know the implant *activated* in the claiming. Treats the bond as the simplest joy of his life — though after S05's crack and Thaal's S06 question, *the simplest joy* has begun to cost him an effort he does not name.
- As of S05 he has asked Mira directly what the thing is and accepted her truthful *I don't know.* **As of S06, he has not asked her about her own subservience** — he has answered to Thaal that *the rest she has done herself,* and has not put the question to her. He does not know she has answered it differently for herself.
- Does **not** know the true total number of women.
