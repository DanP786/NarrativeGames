# Eshen

## Status
Alive. Vahn-rii child, ~8 years old. Lives in the camp; ranges it freely (*"nobody watches me"*). First real interaction with Mira: Day 2 (S03), at the cord of Korren's lodge.

## Description
Thin and quick, all elbows and ready feet. Grey-green skin a shade paler than Korren's; the natural tracery on her arms not yet bright enough to show by daylight. Frank, fearless, enormously curious — too young to have learned the camp's careful adult faces. The bonefern-carrying child glimpsed at Korren's fire in S02.

## Voice & mannerisms
High, certain, decisive in the abrupt way of the young. Reasons things to the bottom and announces the conclusion. Watches with her whole attention. No fear of the strange new woman — only interest.

## Relationship to Mira
**Friendly (provisional).** Watched Mira clean the stored water and revised her estimate of the new woman sharply upward (*"You made it change… with Korren's cord"*). Agreed to fetch sand and char for the water-work; treats the errand as plainly good and therefore not a secret. Her parting question — *"Are you going to make all our water like that? The whole camp's?"* — is, so far, the purest expression of the thing Mira half-wants: to be *for* this place by her own hands.

## Goals & pressures
- A child's: be useful, be near the interesting thing, have a job that's hers.
- (None political — yet. A child is a clean channel into the camp, which cuts both ways.)

## History with Mira
- S02: carried a stem of pale bonefern past Korren's fire (unremarked at the time).
- S03 (Day 2, late morning): stood at the cord watching Mira fix the water; gave her name; told Mira the Vahn-rii **source** their water rather than treat it (*"the river is clean above the camp and dirty below, and we draw above… nobody good drinks standing water"*); decided fetching materials was no secret (*"secrets are for when you did a bad thing"*); ran off for sand, with cold char to come from **Vesh** at the cook-fire (*"he likes me"*). **Currently away on that errand — will return up the terrace path.**

## Knowledge
- Knows the everyday workings of the camp a child knows: who's at which fire, who's kind, where the river is drawn, that Korren has told the new woman to stay in the lodge.
- Knows Mira can make foul water clean — and is openly impressed by it. Will say so if asked; this is how Mira's water-skill may first reach the wider camp / Senna.
- Does **not** know anything of the implant, the verdict, the caves, or what passed between the adults at the cord.
