## HARD CANON — human count
**There are exactly SIX human women on Vethaal. No more, ever.** Four were first found in/around the Vahn-rii camp (Mira + the fighter + the dissociated one + the watcher); the last two (Ada + Mari) were unaccounted for until S05, when Drenn brought them in. All six are now located. The Vahn-rii cannot know the true number — Mira told Thaal "thousands" (about the *ship*), and the tribe reasonably fears more may fall — but the real total is fixed at six. Never introduce a seventh.

## NOTE — all six women carry a nape translation-implant (= the spore route; Suvi's is at the temple). Women understand Kael'tha; the chip-less men do NOT understand human speech (woman-to-woman is private). Only Mira knows she's chipped (Ada & Mari now know they have a neck-scar, not what it is).
