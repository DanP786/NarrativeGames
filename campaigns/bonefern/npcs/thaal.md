# Thaal

## Status
Alive. Chief of the Vahn-rii (river-walker tribe). **Twelve years** in the chiefdom (declared S06). On Day 9 night he **claimed Ada** by plain power at her neck — no cave, no bond; the dark hasn't touched her. As of S06 morning he has been forced by Korren in front of his own council to **take Ada to the rock** if the claim is to bind, and has accepted the price (Korren will publicly answer for Yelka via Vora before the smoke turns black). Tactically diminished by the morning; not unmoored.

## Description
**Broader than Korren and a hand shorter.** Bare torso in warming air. **A long collar-scar at the base of his throat.** His own bead cord — older, darker, more stones in it than Korren's — worn **at the throat** (the male / chief placement, distinct from a mate's cord-at-waist). One broad bare foot scarred across the instep, planted at the fire when he sits.

## Voice & mannerisms
The **dropped-low voice of a chief who does not raise it because he does not have to.** Two registers:
- **Formal voice** — the binding voice of council, used for the morning's actual ritual business.
- **Working voice** — conversational; the same body in the same setting but the formal voice put down. Tactically deployed to ask questions a male would not have to answer on formal grounds.

Long held silences he lets sit *the way a chief does.* Hand opens and closes once on the knee when he is holding what he is feeling. **Addresses Korren as *brother*** — tribal honorific from chief to second; the word is cold when he chooses it.

## Relationship to Mira
**Neutral, tactically interested.** Has not addressed her directly — *if he is wise* (Korren's read), and Thaal was wise. Reads her surface — silent, hands kept, eyes down, cord at her waist, anchored to her male — as **the model of the bonded mate he wants for himself with Ada.** Asked Korren plainly *how* this was made. Did not believe Korren's answer; did not disbelieve it; **filed it.** Acknowledged her at the council's close by a small tilt of the head **at the cord, not at her face** — chief's neutral nod to a mated wife at the close of a council in front of his lodge.

## Goals & pressures
- **Bind Ada to himself by whatever form the camp will accept.** Forced by Korren's invocation back onto the rock; *watch me;* the resistance is lethal-real.
- **Break Ada into Mira's behavioural pattern** — named openly in council that this is his intent.
- Restore the standing he privately feels he lost last night and this morning (Korren forced the lodge entry in S05, used the sacred frame against him, and has just bent custom against him in his own council).
- Use whatever the morning gives him. **The Yelka grievance was a gift; he used it without lifting his voice.**
- Keep the chief's-second function intact (he needs Korren's mouth at his shoulder; the camp listens to Korren after Thaal has finished moving).

## History with the player
- **Day 1:** received Korren's controlled account of Mira's claim; accepted; no further enquiry.
- **S05 (Day 9, terrace):** held the reckoning of *what has been brought into this camp;* upheld Senna's verdict on the made-things; **claimed Ada by power** as the terrace dispersed; deferred his unfinished business with Korren to morning — *"you and I are not done."*
- **S06 (Day 10 morning) — the council in front of his lodge:**
  - Opened to Korren only: *"You have decided what we are going to talk about before I am out of my furs."*
  - Demanded Korren stand at his shoulder for a saying over Ada; **was given the rock back instead.** Conceded the form rather than break custom in his own council. *"You give me the rock back. Twelve years I have been chief of this camp."*
  - Let Yelka's intrusion play out without intervening — used her grievance.
  - Pivoted: **rock for Ada, in return for Korren's public acknowledgement to Yelka via Vora before the smoke turns black.** *"You give me the rock. I give you the dust."*
  - Asked the question of the morning: *"How did you make her so."* Itemised Mira openly; named his intent to do the same with Ada.
  - Filed Korren's non-answer without committing — *"we will see."*
  - Dismissed the council; acknowledged Mira at the cord, not her face; turned away into his lodge.

- Knows Korren claimed Mira at the rock and bonded; knows Drenn↔Mari is fresh; knows his own claim on Ada **is not yet bound.**
