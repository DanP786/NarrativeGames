# Yelka

## Status
Alive. Young Vahn-rii woman of the camp; **unmated** (no cord at her waist). **Was promised to / expected for Korren** before his claim of Mira at the rock — whether by formal arrangement or strong communal expectation is not yet known. S06 morning: broke the chief's council to confront Mira and Korren publicly, snatched at Mira's mate-cord; Korren pried her off without breaking her. Walked out of the lower terrace under her own dignity. Met at the lodges' edge by her elder, **Vora,** who walked her out of sight. **Korren has been ordered by Thaal to make her a public acknowledgement before the smoke turns black.**

## Description
Slim, dark. **Temple-tattoos at brow and cheekbone** (Vahn-rii woman's markings). Long hair worn loose down her back this morning — *the way of someone who has not bothered with it.* Old enough to throw Thaal's own *twelve years* back in his mouth — implies tenure or at least old-camp standing. **No bead cord at her waist** — the absence is what reads, in the camp, as still-unmated / set-aside.

## Voice & mannerisms
Raised voice when broken, but **does not rant — short sharp lines, formal Vahn-rii structure under the anger** (*"He allows this. He allows it."*). Eyes wet and dry at the same time of a woman past crying. **The chin-lift of a woman who has been put down** — the bearing of her own people's grief, preserved in posture. Did not give Mira eye-engagement after losing the cord; **looked at the cord, not the face** — preserved what she could.

## Relationship to Mira / Korren
**Hostile (Korren); hostile-distant (Mira).** The wrong is Korren's (he set her down for the new woman); Mira is the *face* of what was done to her. May, given a proper acknowledgement, accept the form and stand down from open enmity. The wound is fresh; today she will not be moved further than dignity allows.

## Goals & pressures
- **Have the camp acknowledge what was done to her.** This morning, in front of the council, she made the case; the chief has put the obligation on Korren.
- **Preserve what standing she has in the camp's eyes.** Walking out of the terrace under her own dignity, on Vora's arm, was the first move of that preservation.
- (Private — to be discovered:) what she actually wants from Korren's acknowledgement — *a saying, a gift of standing, a name spoken before witnesses.* TBD when seen.

## History with the player
- **Pre-S01 / camp life:** a young woman of the camp Korren was expected to take. Not pursued by him; not yet rejected by him before the abductions either. Then the human women came, and Korren chose Mira at the rock.
- **S06 (Day 10 morning):** broke the chief's council; *"He was to be mine"* in front of Mira; **snatched at the cord at Mira's waist — the single knot Korren tied held;** was pried off finger-by-finger and let go of her own arm; *"You let me down in front of the whole camp before the sun was up, and now you stand at his fire and tell him he must take **her** to the rock — her — and not me."* Looked at the cord, not Mira's face, on the way out.

## Knowledge
- Knows the broad camp politics. Knows the new women came not by their own feet; knows Mira is bonded to Korren.
- **Does NOT know about the made-things at the women's napes** (the spore route is unknown to her).
- Does NOT know the human women's origin beyond rumour. **Does NOT know the true count of six.**
