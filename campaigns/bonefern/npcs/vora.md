# Vora

## Status
Alive. Older Vahn-rii woman of the camp. **Yelka's elder** — kin or clan-mother; the older woman who came forward at the edge of the lodges, put an arm round Yelka, and walked her out of sight at the close of the morning's council interruption. **Named in council by Thaal** to be the woman before whom Korren must make his acknowledgement to Yelka *before the smoke turns black.* Standing enough that the chief names her directly.

## Description
Older. Mira saw her only briefly — **knew her by her step and by the set of her cord that she was *someone.*** A **mate-cord at her waist** (woman *taken to the rock, brought back, named*). Did not speak during her appearance in S06 — picked Yelka up and walked her off the terrace with the quiet authority of a woman who does not need to.
