# Drenn

## Status
Alive. Senior Vahn-rii scout, axe-bearer. Ranking enough to speak at the chief's fire; the longest bead-count in the camp (the deep places, many times). Day 2: faced down by Korren at his own fire. **S05: tracked Korren & Mira's forbidden run all the way into the sacred caves — and was himself BONDED there, against his will, to one of the two new women he'd gathered off the trail (Mari). He has CLAIMED her, accepting Korren's gift-frame, and brought her home. The man sent to bring the defiant pair to heel returned changed and on the wrong side of his own chief.**

## Description
Older than Korren — perhaps a generation. Broader, heavier. Darker bronze, weathered. Bioluminescent traceries faded/broken where old scars cross them (lit unevenly in S05 — an old light not used in years). A long bone-white slash along one thigh. Heavy hafted axe; close-blade at the hip. **Bone-bead necklace longer than Thaal's — the longest in the camp.**

## Voice & mannerisms
Slow. Economical. Wastes no word. Announces himself by name when about to use his standing — the formal *"Drenn"* is his standing-up-from-his-seat. Never raises his voice. Small disapproving sounds (through teeth/throat); a shorter through-the-nose sound — grim satisfaction — when a thing he suspected is confirmed. **S05 added a new register to him: a man hearing his own certainties fail in his mouth** (*"I do not know what this is"*) — the most measured man in camp, unmade in the open.

## Relationship to Mira
**Wary — but now entangled with, and reliant on, her.** His Day-2 read of her as *"the kind of thing"* and his parting prophecy (*"Keep his rule. It will not keep you."*) both came true in S05 — Korren's arm was between them and it was not enough; the deep places reached in. In the caves he turned to Mira for answers (*"What is this thing. You know — I can see it on your face"*), and she handed the question back to him (*"This is your planet. Your ritual."*). He no longer simply distrusts her; he suspects she understands what is happening to all of them, and he wants it. That need is a lever Korren has already used.

## Relationship to Korren
Senior; professional; the cold standoff of Day 2 **inverted in S05**: sent to bring Korren to heel, Drenn ended up standing *with* him at the terrace, because Korren handed him the only frame that lets him keep his beads and his new bond both — *call it the gift, not a sickness; not a fooling.* Korren read him not as a rival but as *a problem that handed over its own solution,* and Drenn's standing (the chief's word, the right to make Korren answer) guttered along with his light. The "unsurfaced thing" between them is now buried under a debt: Drenn cannot press the reckoning without unravelling the legitimacy of his own claim on Mari.

## Relationship to Mari
**Newly bonded / claiming.** The dark took him helplessly, mid-reprimand, the pull dragging his feet across a clearing he didn't choose to cross — and Korren's "gift" reframe gave him permission to stop fighting it. He brings her by the nape (*keep her close, keep her quiet, carry her if she won't walk*), drawn to her distress the way the bonded are drawn. The gentleness and the ownership are grown into one thing. To Drenn this is the deep places choosing for him; he does not know it is a spore, and the not-choosing of it shamed him until Korren told him it was holy.

## Goals & pressures
- Reconcile the bond that took him with the self-governed man he has always been (the open wound of S05).
- Keep Mari; make sense of what the dark did to him.
- Get answers — increasingly from Mira, the woman who *knows.*
- Preserve what standing he can after returning compromised.

## Knowledge
- Knows the territory and the deep places intimately (longest bead-count).
- Knows Korren defied chief and matriarch, reached the rock, and is bonded; was there for Senna finding the made-thing on Mari and her *"a hand wearing the deep places like a skin"* — so he now half-holds the women-were-made-for-this suspicion, even as the gift-frame papers over it for him.
- Suspects Mira understands the mechanism and is withholding it.
- Does **not** know the true number of women; does **not** consciously know the spore or the implants' function — frames it all as the deep places, now turned inward on himself.
