# The Vahn-rii males — the four who found the women

Shared: tall, powerful grey-green humanoids; pebbled skin; slit amber eyes; fanged jaw; retractable claws; bioluminescent tracery down arms/sternum that brightens with feeling; minimal river-tribe hide clothing. (Reference art on file, four males L→R = Korren, Vesh, Asho, Tarrik.)

## Korren — river-walker scout (keeper of Mira)
Lean, athletic swimmer's build; long pointed ears; amber eyes; wet near-black hair swept back; **green-teal** bioluminescent tracery down chest and arms; bare-chested, brown hide hip-wrap and cord belt. The clean-limbed, handsome one. Low chest-hum (his tell). *Full sheet: `npcs/korren.md`.*

## Vesh — keeper of Ren (Korren's younger brother)
Massive, heavily muscled, near-orcish; darker grey skin, heavy brow, broad blunt features, thick neck; glow sparse and dim; coarse hide chest-strap and a heavy layered hip-wrap; scarred; immovable. **Korren's younger brother.** Holds the fighter bound but untouched, waiting for her to be a person before he decides anything.

## Asho — keeper of Liesel
Slimmer, refined, younger-seeming; smooth grey skin; pointed ears; **blue** tracery; a beaded necklace and woven crossed shoulder-strap; tidy wrap with hanging beads; a gentle, almost uncertain face. A male who expected a wife and got a woman who isn't there.

## Tarrik — keeper of Suvi
Lean, watchful, clean-limbed; **amber/gold** tracery; sharp controlled features; crossed strap and a **russet/red-brown** hide wrap; alert and contained — the watcher's mirror.
