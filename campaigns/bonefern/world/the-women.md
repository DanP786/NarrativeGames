# The six human women — the Helia-Voss drop

**HARD CANON: six total.** First four into Vahn-rii hands (found Day 1): Mira, Ren, Liesel, Suvi. The last two (Ada, Mari) were brought into the camp by Drenn in S05 — **all six are now located and in Vahn-rii hands.** All adults. All likely carry a sealed nape-implant by the same hand — **except Suvi, whose mark sits visible at the cheekbone/temple,** which may mean her role was built different. (Reference art on file.)

## Mira Holloway — 26, life-support & filtration tech (PC, with Korren)
Long black wet wavy hair; warm brown skin; soft, full figure; large dark expressive eyes; full mouth. Wears the dust-red wrap Korren re-cut to her body, his mark left unwashed beneath it. Nape-implant hidden under the hair. The softness Senna thinks she was built around.

## Ren Castellanos — 28, ag-deck security (with Vesh) — THE FIGHTER
Short dark hair; strong, muscled athletic build; olive/tan skin; hard lean arms; tough, wary face. Dark-brown asymmetric hide wrap cinched at the hip; forearm bindings; rope-burned wrists. Came up swinging on the moss and hasn't stopped.

## Liesel Varga — 30, ship's botanist (with Asho) — THE ABSENT ONE
Fair, pale skin; blonde hair braided up; serene-blank, dissociated gaze. Fully dressed in a cream/white crossed-shoulder robe by Asho's household — composed, cared-for, and no one home behind her face. Her compliance is the unsettling kind: no one there to consent.

## Suvi Lindqvist — 25, navigation/logistics analyst (with Tarrik) — THE WATCHER
Copper/auburn-red hair pulled back; freckled fair skin; sharp light eyes; a small geometric mark on the cheekbone/temple, **placed to be seen.** Rust/red-brown one-shoulder wrap, worn precisely. Counts everything; recognised Mira at the fire (they traded one look). Senna's instinct: most likely *aimed* — a quiet eye set down inside a tribe.

## Ada Okonkwo — 31, structural engineer — IN THE VAHN-RII CAMP (CLAIMED BY THAAL)
Tall, statuesque; deep-brown skin; tight close cornrows; lean, hard engineer's muscle; **reddish coolant-burn scar up the right forearm**; calm, guarded; big capable hands. **As of S05, brought in off the trail by Drenn and CLAIMED by the chief Thaal** — a power/custom claim at her neck, **not** a spore-bond; she is unbonded, the only one in the party the cave has not touched. Clear-eyed, resisting (*watch me*); knows she has a neck-scar but not what it is. As of S06 she is at the silt-bank below camp after fleeing toward the cliff bend and being pulled from the river by Mira and Korren (see `chronicle/`).

## Mari Quintero — 23, galley hand & agronomy apprentice — IN THE VAHN-RII CAMP (BONDED TO & CLAIMED BY DRENN)
Youngest. Warm golden-brown skin; auburn-brown curls in wet ringlets; rounder youthful face; fuller figure; wary, uncertain. **As of S05, brought in off the trail by Drenn; the cave bonded her to him through her nape-implant and he has CLAIMED her** — she hums back involuntarily, terrified. Kept at the women's fires under Senna's eye. Knows she has a neck-scar but not what it is.
