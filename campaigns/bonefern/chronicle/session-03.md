# Session 03 — 25 May 2026 (real-world)

*(Day 2 on Vethaal, late morning → night. Resumes where S02 left off — Senna at the cord of Korren's lodge — and ends with Mira and Korren slipping the camp for the forbidden caves.)*

## Scene 1 — Korren's lodge / the entry cord, then Mira alone (Day 2, late morning)

**The exam and the verdict.** Senna refused to be turned away; Korren stood aside only when Mira laid a hand on his shoulder — *"We will have all evening."* While the two held each other at the door, Mira quietly cleaned herself with the herb-water, wiping away even the mark Korren had said she'd "wear all of today," and redressed (a private self-assertion; Korren clocked the bare clean shoulder and banked it). For the exam Mira turned *into* Korren, keeping his hand, baring her nape. Verdict: the implant is **awake** — and it warmed not when she *spoke* but when she *turned into him.* *"It is not your speaking it answers to."* It wakes to his nearness. Senna forbade the caves while it is awake and named she'd carry the verdict to Thaal.

**The cut, and what it revealed.** Korren: *"Then we take it out."* Senna refused to his face — it sits against the spine, a finger's width from paralysis, a breath from death; a blind cut on an unknown make would likely kill her. The danger to Mira's body did **not** loosen his grip. What set the knife down was Mira's *"We wouldn't understand each other, we'd lose our language… you'd keep me, and you couldn't ask me a single thing."* Senna made it a lesson: *"the cut might kill you and he held you the tighter; you tell him he would not get to talk to you — and he sets the knife down… it will tell you what you are."* She laid the morning's withheld warning on his own floor: *"That is the very thing I told her this morning you would not be able to tell apart from keeping her."*

**The price of permission.** Mira, shy: *"What will it take? For us to have permission?"* Senna's answer: know what the awake thing *does* (sleeper, or an *eye* that shows the seeing-dark's findings to whoever made her); she can't cut it (lethal) or read an unknown make, and the one who'd know was cut so she couldn't — so it may take long or be impossible. She offered the **slow sanctioned road**: days of watching; if it proves no eye, she opens the rock herself, properly, with Thaal's and the women's word. Fork drawn to Korren's face: her road (*"through the dark and still hers"*) vs his (*"faster, and his, and it ends somewhere I cannot see"*).

**Korren's choice; the kiss.** He chose to walk Senna to Thaal himself, saying nothing of why. Kissed Mira slow and deep first — *like a man setting down a thing he means to come back for.* *"Stay. I will not be long."*

**Mira alone — the water and Eshen.** Unwatched for the first time since the moss, Mira nearly went to find the other women, got to the cord, and **chose to obey *stay.*** She tidied the lodge, then made the stale stored water her project — diagnosed it, ran the cold-process she could, hit the wall (needs heat / char + sand, all outside), and refused to dose it with unknown botanicals. The watching child, **Eshen** (~8), agreed to fetch sand and char; revealed the Vahn-rii *source* water rather than treat it (*"clean above the camp, dirty below… nobody good drinks standing water"*); told *"you decide"* about secrecy, reasoned *"secrets are for when you did a bad thing"* and chose openness. Parting question, landing on Mira's want: *"Are you going to make all our water like that? The whole camp's?"*

## Scene 2 — Korren's lodge: the return, the decision, the departure (Day 2, midday → night)

**The water finished; Korren returns changed.** Eshen brought sand + char (Vesh gave it with an unheard question — Vesh now half-aware). Mira built a proper char→sand→cord filter and produced **genuinely clean water,** lining the bowls in work-order. Korren returned from Thaal with the hum *flat/held* (the dangerous register), took in the made-domestic lodge *"like a man watching the tide come in over ground he thought was his,"* eyes going again to the cleaned shoulder. He delivered the verdict flatly: **the caves are formally shut** — Thaal upheld Senna; only her days-of-watching road remains. **(CANON, off-screen: Korren also asked Thaal leave to take Mira fully *before* the caves and was REFUSED — the rule is not the chief's to unmake. Mira does NOT know he asked or was refused; she reads only the shape of a man told *no.*)** Doubly walled, only the secret nightfall run left as a fast route.

**The water moves him; the want sharpens.** Mira showed him the filtered water — *"it's what I used to do on the ship"* — and named her one fear (*I didn't leave*). Korren drank it, confirmed it cleaner, and reframed her competence as possession: *"It makes you worse to lose… every new thing you show me makes the dark I want to take you into harder to wait for."* First time he named the cleaned mark aloud — mild, a fact, not an accusation.

**The choice, made hers.** Mira leaned in (unknowing of the angle she gave him) — *"Are we still going tonight?"* Korren refused to decide over the top of her again: laid out the full cost (against chief and matriarch both, no asking twice, return possibly unsurvivable), admitted he wants it more and for the *worse* reasons (Senna's fear-not-keeping warning, which *"she is so rarely wrong"*), and made Mira choose with her eyes open. She pressed — *what is in the caves; we're together now, they haven't taken me* — and he answered with the truth as his people hold it: **no one knows what's in them; you don't go to *see*, you go because the deep places give or withhold the bond; the rock looks at the two of you and decides.** (He does not know it is the spore.) He named the caves plainly for what they are *to him*: not a marvel — *"a door I can close behind us,"* the one place no far hand can reach down and lift her off the moss a second time. Then: *"ask me again, if you still mean it… all the way down."* Mira: **"We go."**

**The waiting, and Suvi's reach.** Decision sealed (*"a thing decided is carried quiet"*). Mira followed his instructions obediently — and, by her own admission to herself, *likes* his possessiveness; it makes her feel safe and cared-for after a life of being a uniform nobody looked at (she half-knows this pull may be the maker's design and cannot make herself care). They waited out the afternoon close. Then **Eshen returned**: the red-haired woman across camp — **Suvi** — asked, through the child, *"how you made the bad water good," "and only you,"* using **Mira's real name** that no one here was given. Mira deflected (*"in the morning"*; Korren relayed it for her when her whisper didn't carry). Korren marked the danger: Suvi has shown the listening camp that she and Mira share a private tongue, on the very night he means to take Mira out of her reach.

**The departure.** Mira nodded off; Korren woke her after dark, the fires banked low, his light dimmed, gear already packed (nothing of hers to carry). He led her out past the cord, low and silent, along the terrace and up into the canopy — *"match my feet… if I put you down behind something, make yourself small and wait, I will come back for you. You will not lose me in this."* Mira followed quietly. The firelight fell behind; the dark took them both. **The forbidden run is executing.**
